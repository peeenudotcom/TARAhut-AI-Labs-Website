## 1. Program Summary

The AI Power 8-Week Program transforms non-IT students into AI-confident professionals who can use 15+ AI tools to solve real-world problems, build portfolios, and start freelancing. Designed for BBA, B.Com, BA, and IELTS/study-abroad aspirants in Punjab, the program follows a 20-40-40 pedagogy (20% theory, 40% guided practice, 40% independent work) delivered in a mix of English, Hindi, and Punjabi.

By the end of 8 weeks, every student will have:
- Mastery of 15+ AI tools across writing, design, video, automation, and chatbots
- A live portfolio website showcasing real projects
- Active freelancing profiles on Fiverr and Upwork
- A capstone project completed for a real local business
- A certificate of completion from TARAhut AI Labs

**Price:** INR 9,999 (inclusive of all materials, tool access guidance, and certificate)

## 4. Standard Session Format (120 Minutes)

Every session follows this exact structure. Trainers must not deviate without centre-head approval.

| Block | Duration | Activity | Pedagogy Split |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Warm-Up | 5 min | Energy activity, fun AI fact, or quick quiz | -- |
| 2. Recap | 10 min | Review previous session; students share one takeaway each | 20% Theory |
| 3. Instruction | 25 min | New concept taught with live demo on projector | 20% Theory |
| 4. Guided Practice | 30 min | Students follow along step-by-step with trainer | 40% Guided |
| 5. Break | 5 min | Screen break, stretch, chai allowed | -- |
| 6. Independent Work | 35 min | Students work on tasks/projects; trainer circulates | 40% Independent |
| 7. Wrap-Up | 10 min | 3 students share work; trainer previews next session; homework assigned | -- |
| **Total** | **120 min** | | |

## 13. Pedagogy Philosophy: 20-40-40

```
20% THEORY          40% GUIDED PRACTICE       40% INDEPENDENT WORK
(Warm-Up + Recap    (Trainer does, students    (Students do, trainer
 + Instruction)      follow along)              supports)

"Main samjhaata     "Mere saath karo"          "Ab tum karo, main
 hoon"                                          help karunga"
```

**Why this works for non-IT students:**
- They see before they do (reduces anxiety)
- Guided practice builds muscle memory with safety net
- Independent work builds real confidence and portfolio pieces
- Every session produces something tangible -- no "theory-only" days

## 14. Language Guidelines

- **Technical terms** in English (prompt, chatbot, automation, workflow)
- **Explanations** in Hindi/Punjabi ("Isko samjho aise..." / "Eh samjho iddaan...")
- **Slides** in English with Hindi translations for key terms
- **Student submissions** accepted in any language
- **Encourage** students to practice English in prompts (improves AI output quality)
