# AI for Digital Marketing -- Trainer Manual

## Program Overview

**Course Title:** AI for Digital Marketing
**Provider:** TARAhut AI Labs / Uplrn AI Labs
**Location:** Punjab, India (franchise-ready for any center)
**Duration:** 12 Weeks (48 Sessions)
**Schedule:** 4 days/week, 2 hours per session (120 minutes)
**Price:** INR 24,999
**Language:** English / Hindi / Punjabi mix
**Pedagogy:** 20-40-40 (20% Instruction, 40% Guided Practice, 40% Independent Work)

---

## Program Philosophy

Students pick a REAL local business on Day 1 and spend 12 weeks building everything for that business -- Google Business Profile, social media content, ad campaigns, funnels, automation, and reporting. By Week 12 they walk out with a portfolio, paying clients, and a clear income roadmap. This is not theory. This is a working digital marketing agency in 12 weeks.

## Prerequisites

### Student Prerequisites
- Basic computer literacy (can use a web browser, type, create accounts)
- A smartphone with WhatsApp
- A laptop or desktop with internet access (minimum 10 Mbps)
- Gmail account
- No prior marketing or AI experience required -- this course starts from ZERO
- Willingness to approach and work with a real local business

### Recommended (Not Required)
- Basic English reading ability (trainer delivers in Hindi/Punjabi but tools are in English)
- Access to a phone with a decent camera (for content creation)

---

## Trainer Requirements

### Qualifications
- Minimum 1 year hands-on experience running digital marketing campaigns
- Proficiency with Meta Ads Manager (must have managed at least INR 50,000 in ad spend)
- Working knowledge of all tools in the tool stack (see below)
- Completed the Uplrn Trainer Certification Program (internal)
- Comfortable teaching in English/Hindi/Punjabi

### Trainer Responsibilities
- Follow the session plans EXACTLY as written (franchise consistency)
- Arrive 15 minutes before each session for setup
- Review each student's real-business project weekly
- Maintain the Student Progress Tracker (shared Google Sheet)
- Hold weekly 1-on-1 check-ins with struggling students (Wednesday buffer day)
- Submit weekly trainer report to center manager
- NEVER skip guided practice or independent work time -- the doing IS the learning

### Trainer-to-Student Ratio
- Maximum 15 students per batch
- If batch exceeds 12, a teaching assistant is required
