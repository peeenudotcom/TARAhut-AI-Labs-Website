# Phase 4: Business Building & Certification -- Sessions 37-48 (Weeks 10-12)

## Phase 4 Overview

**Duration:** 3 Weeks, 12 Sessions
**Goal:** Students transition from practitioners to business owners. They set up automation, CRM, and reporting systems. They package their services, create pricing, build a portfolio, and acquire their first paying clients. The course culminates in a final presentation, certification ceremony, and a clear 30-60-90 day income roadmap.
**Key Milestone:** By end of Week 12, every student has a service package, a portfolio website, at least one client prospect (ideally their course client converting to a paid engagement), and a Certified AI Digital Marketing Professional certificate.

---

# WEEK 10: Automation, CRM & Reporting

## Week 10 Learning Objectives
- Set up email marketing campaigns with Mailchimp
- Use social media scheduling tools to batch-manage content
- Configure a CRM system (Zoho CRM or HubSpot) for client/lead management
- Build workflow automations with Make.com or Zapier
- Create professional client reports and invoicing systems

## Week 10 Deliverable
**Automation Workflow + CRM Setup** -- A live Mailchimp email campaign (3-email welcome sequence), a scheduled week of social media content, a CRM with client data entered, at least 1 automation workflow (Make.com/Zapier), a client report template, and an invoicing setup.

# WEEK 11: Client Acquisition & Business Launch

## Week 11 Learning Objectives
- Package services into tiered offerings (INR 5K, 10K, 25K/month)
- Create a professional rate card and proposal template
- Master client outreach through WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, walk-in pitches, and cold outreach
- Build a portfolio website or profile page
- Set up profiles on Fiverr, Upwork, and LinkedIn for client acquisition

## Week 11 Deliverable
**Client Pitch Deck + Pricing Proposal** -- A professional pitch deck (10-12 slides), a rate card with 3 service tiers, a portfolio website (or Linktree/Carrd profile), active profiles on 2+ freelance platforms, and evidence of at least 3 real outreach attempts to prospective clients.
