# Phase 1: Foundations -- Sessions 1-12 (Weeks 1-3)

## Phase 1 Overview

**Duration:** 3 Weeks, 12 Sessions
**Goal:** Students understand AI fundamentals, pick their real business client, learn digital marketing basics, select a niche, and master prompt engineering for marketing use cases.
**Key Milestone:** By end of Week 3, every student has a real local business client and a library of 50+ marketing prompts ready to use.

---

# WEEK 1: AI Fundamentals + Marketing Opportunity

## Week 1 Learning Objectives
- Understand what AI is and how it is transforming marketing
- Create accounts on ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini
- Identify the AI marketing opportunity in India
- Pick a REAL local business to work with for 12 weeks

## Week 1 Deliverable
**AI Tools Exploration Report** -- Written document + 5-minute demo showing what each AI tool can do for marketing. Includes the name and details of their chosen real business client.

### Week 3 Assessment Rubric: Prompt Library (50 Marketing Prompts)

| Criteria | Excellent (5) | Good (3-4) | Needs Work (1-2) |
|----------|--------------|------------|-------------------|
| Quantity | 50+ prompts, well-distributed across categories | 40-49 prompts with some category gaps | Less than 40 prompts |
| Quality | Prompts use RECIPE framework, are specific and tested | Most prompts are functional but some are vague | Many prompts are generic or untested |
| Organization | Clear categories, tags, ratings, and tool recommendations | Basic categorization but missing ratings or tool recs | Disorganized or no categorization |
| Sample Outputs | Every prompt includes a sample output with quality rating | Most prompts have sample outputs | Few or no sample outputs |
| Top 10 Power Prompts | Clearly highlighted, genuinely the best 10, diverse channels | Top 10 selected but not clearly the best | No top 10 or poorly chosen |
| Client Relevance | Many prompts customized for their actual client business | Some client-specific prompts mixed with generic ones | All prompts are generic, none client-specific |
