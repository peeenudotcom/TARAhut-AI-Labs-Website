## Program Summary

This 3-week intensive program goes deep into the mechanics of Large Language Models and teaches advanced prompt engineering techniques used by AI professionals. Participants will understand transformer architecture, master multiple prompting frameworks, build production-ready AI workflows, and create Custom GPTs and Claude Projects for real business applications.

This is not a beginner course. Participants will leave with a professional prompt portfolio, multiple deployed AI tools, and the skills to design AI workflows for any industry.

## Prerequisites

Participants MUST meet the following before enrolling:

1. **Active AI usage:** Must have used ChatGPT or Claude regularly for at least 1 month
2. **Basic prompting ability:** Can write structured prompts (not just simple questions)
3. **Computer literacy:** Comfortable with multiple browser tabs, Google Docs, copying/pasting content
4. **English proficiency:** Comfortable reading and writing in English (course materials are in English; discussion can be bilingual)
5. **Laptop/desktop:** Must bring their own device with reliable internet access
6. **Accounts ready:** ChatGPT account (free or Plus), Claude account (free or Pro), Google account

### Pre-Course Assessment (Mandatory)
Before the first session, participants complete a short online assessment:
- Write a prompt for a specific business scenario (evaluated for structure and specificity)
- Explain in 2-3 sentences what ChatGPT is and how it works
- Share 3 screenshots of their best AI interactions from the past month

Passing score: 60%. Students who score below may be redirected to a beginner course.

## Assessment Framework

### Continuous Assessment (50%)

| Component | Weight | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Session Participation | 10% | Quality of questions, contributions, and experiments |
| Weekly Experiments | 15% | Documented prompt experiments with analysis |
| Prompt Portfolio | 15% | Growing collection of advanced prompts with commentary |
| Peer Review | 10% | Quality of feedback given and received |

### Final Assessment (50%)

| Component | Weight | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Prompt Portfolio | 20% | Complete portfolio: 50+ prompts across all frameworks with analysis |
| Final Project | 30% | Complete AI workflow for a real business use case (presented live) |

### Prompt Portfolio Requirements
- Minimum 50 prompts across all frameworks taught
- Each prompt must include: the framework used, the raw prompt, the AI output, analysis of what worked/did not work, and iteration notes
- Organized by framework and application area
- Demonstrates progression from basic to advanced techniques

### Final Project Requirements
- Solve a real business problem using AI workflows
- Must include: problem statement, AI tools selected, prompt chain documentation, output quality analysis, and ROI estimate
- Live presentation: 5 minutes + 3 minutes Q&A
- Deliverables: working AI workflow (Custom GPT or Claude Project), documentation, presentation
