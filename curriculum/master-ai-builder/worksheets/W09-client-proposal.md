### Proposal Presentation Tips

- Print this or share as a PDF -- it looks more professional
- Always show the Digital Presence Audit Score first -- it creates urgency
- Offer 3 packages -- most clients pick the middle one
- Include 2-3 sample work items (posts, logos) to show what they will get
- Set clear deadlines and payment terms
- Follow up within 48 hours if no response
