### Instructions

Create detailed briefs for AI-generated images. The better your brief, the better the image. Use the Image Prompt Formula to build complete prompts.

### Image Prompt Formula

> **Style** + **Subject** + **Composition** + **Lighting** + **Mood** + **Technical** = Complete Prompt

**Example:** "Photorealistic product photo of a glass of mango lassi on a wooden table, centered composition, warm golden hour sunlight from the left, inviting and fresh mood, shot on Canon 85mm lens, shallow depth of field, 4K resolution"
