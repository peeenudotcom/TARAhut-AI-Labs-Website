### Video Quality Checklist

Before publishing any video, check all items:

- [ ] Hook grabs attention in the first 3 seconds
- [ ] Audio is clear (no background noise)
- [ ] Captions/subtitles are added
- [ ] Background music is added (not too loud)
- [ ] CTA is visible and clear
- [ ] Brand logo or watermark is included
- [ ] Video is exported in 1080p or higher
- [ ] Aspect ratio is correct (9:16 for Reels/Shorts, 16:9 for YouTube, 1:1 for feed)
- [ ] Thumbnail is eye-catching
- [ ] File size is under 100MB for easy sharing
