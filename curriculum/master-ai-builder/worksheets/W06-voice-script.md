### Voice Script Tips

- Use short sentences. They sound more natural when spoken.
- Mark pauses: [PAUSE - 0.5s] for a breath, [PAUSE - 1s] for a beat, [PAUSE - 2s] for drama.
- Use **bold** for words you want the AI voice to emphasize.
- Read your script out loud before generating the voiceover.
- Keep ad scripts under 30 seconds, explainers under 2 minutes.
