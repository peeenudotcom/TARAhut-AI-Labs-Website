## Program Structure

### Month 1: AI Foundations + Tool Mastery (Weeks 1–4, Sessions 1–16)
Build the foundation. Students go from "what is AI?" to confidently using 10+ tools. They build their first 15+ portfolio pieces.

### Month 2: AI Creation + Workflow Building (Weeks 5–8, Sessions 17–32)
Level up to creation. Video, voice, image generation, automation workflows. Students become creators, not just users.

### Month 3: Monetization + Real-World Application (Weeks 9–12, Sessions 33–48)
Make money. Real client projects, freelancing setup, personal brand launch, capstone. Students graduate earning-ready.

## Session Format (Every Session)

Every 120-minute session follows this structure:

| Time | Block | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 0:00–0:05 | Warm-Up | Quick AI news/fact of the day. Energy setter. |
| 0:05–0:15 | Recap & Connect | Review previous session. 2-3 student shares. Connect to today. |
| 0:15–0:40 | Instruction (25 min) | New concept/tool demonstration. Trainer shows, explains, demos. |
| 0:40–1:10 | Guided Practice (30 min) | Students follow along on their devices. Trainer walks the room. |
| 1:10–1:15 | Break (5 min) | |
| 1:15–1:50 | Independent Work (35 min) | Students work on exercises/projects. Trainer assists individually. |
| 1:50–2:00 | Wrap-Up (10 min) | Key takeaways. Preview next session. Homework assignment. |

**Total: 25 min instruction + 30 min guided + 35 min independent = 20%/33%/39% (close to 20-40-40)**
