## Program Summary

AI Explorer Senior is a 4-week intensive program designed for Class 8-10 students who want to harness AI tools for academic excellence and future career readiness. The course bridges the gap between board exam preparation and emerging AI skills, giving students a competitive edge in both academics and the rapidly evolving job market.

Students will learn prompt engineering fundamentals, use AI for board exam preparation (CBSE/ICSE), build their own chatbots, create AI-powered content, and explore future career paths in AI -- all through hands-on, project-based learning.

## Assessment Framework

### Continuous Assessment (60%)

| Component | Weight | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Session Participation | 10% | Engagement, questions, contributions |
| Weekly Assignments | 20% | Take-home AI challenges (one per week) |
| In-Class Activities | 15% | Guided and independent practice outputs |
| Peer Review | 15% | Feedback given and received during sessions |

### Final Assessment (40%)

| Component | Weight | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Practical Exam | 20% | Live prompt engineering challenge (30 min timed test) |
| Project Showcase | 20% | Present chatbot + AI study plan to parents/teachers |

### Practical Exam Details
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Format: Students receive 5 real-world scenarios and must write effective prompts
- Scoring: Clarity (25%), Specificity (25%), Output Quality (25%), Creativity (25%)
- Passing score: 60%

### Project Showcase Details
- Duration: 5 minutes per student
- Format: Live demo of chatbot + presentation of AI study workflow
- Audience: Parents, teachers, and peers
- Scoring rubric: Functionality (30%), Presentation (30%), Creativity (20%), Practical Value (20%)
