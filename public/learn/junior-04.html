Where to Make AI Art?

Canva (canva.com) — Free! Has "Magic Design" and "Text to Image" that creates pictures from your words.
Microsoft Bing Image Creator — Also free! Very powerful and easy to use. 🎨

💡 Art Prompt Tip!

Add style words to make your art amazing: cartoon, realistic, watercolour, colourful, dark, bright, cute, magical — each word changes the whole look! 🌈

Create Your Own Art! 🖼️

Step-by-step guide to making AI art in Canva — and a prompt builder just for you!

📋 Step-by-Step: Make Art in Canva

1

Open Canva

Go to canva.com and sign up for free (ask a parent to help!). Click "Create a Design" → choose "Poster" or "Instagram Post".

2

Click "Magic Design" or "Apps" → "Text to Image"

Look for the ✨ magic icon in the left sidebar, or go to Apps and find "Text to Image". This is where AI draws for you!

3

Type What You Want!

Describe your picture in the box. Be specific! Add colours, style (cartoon/realistic), characters, and background.

4

Click "Generate" and Wait!

Canva creates 4 different versions of your image in about 10 seconds! Pick your favourite. 🌟

5

Download and Share!

Click "Download" to save your art. Send it on WhatsApp to family — they'll be amazed you made it! 📤
