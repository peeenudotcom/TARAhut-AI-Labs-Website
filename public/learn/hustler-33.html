Section 01 — Quality Final

Final Quality Check

Trainer reviews EVERY student's deliverables. Nothing goes to a client that is not professional quality. Be brutal on quality today. This represents YOUR reputation.

"Aaj ka din tumhari zindagi ka sabse important din ho sakta hai. Aaj tum pehli baar apni mehnat ka paisa kamaoge. AI se. Khud se. Professional ban ke."

Deliverable Quality Checklist

  • Designs professional? Colors match, fonts readable, no pixelation, logo placed correctly
  • Captions error-free? No typos, proper grammar, natural Hindi-English mix
  • GMB description optimized? Keywords included, under 750 chars, accurate info
  • Delivery document organized? Easy for client to understand, everything labeled
  • Invoice ready? Amount correct, UPI details clear, payment terms stated
⚠️

Fix Issues NOW

"Yeh post client ko nahi bhej sakte. Color match nahi ho raha. Fix karo, 5 minute mein." Better to delay 10 minutes than deliver sloppy work. Pehla impression sale pe aata hai. Doosra impression delivery pe.

The 15-Minute Client Handover

A structured handover builds trust for the next month. Follow this exact structure.

STEP 1🙏

"Thank You for Choosing Us"

30 seconds of genuine gratitude. "Aapka bahut shukriya ki aapne humpe trust kiya. Hum aapke business ke liye bahut achha kaam kiya hai."

STEP 2📋

"Here Is What We Created"

5 minutes walking through each deliverable. Show every post, every GMB optimization, every WhatsApp setup. Let them see the volume and quality.

STEP 3📖

"Here Is How to Use It"

3 minutes explaining the posting schedule, how to share content, when to post. Make it easy for them to use what you created.

STEP 4📅

"Your Next Month Plan"

2 minutes previewing what comes next (for retainer clients). "Agle mahine yeh festivals hain, yeh offers plan karenge."

STEP 5💬

"Feedback?"

2 minutes asking: "Aapko kaisa laga? Koi changes chahiye?" Handle revision requests gracefully: "Bilkul, kal tak fix karke bhej dunga."

STEP 6-7💰

Invoice + Referral Ask

1 minute: share invoice, request payment. Then: "Ek request -- aapko achha laga toh 2-3 aur business owners ko refer kar dijiye. Aur Google pe ek review de dijiye."

🔑

If Client Asks for Revisions

"Bilkul, main kal tak fix karke bhej dunga." Never argue with a client about their preferences. Make the change and move on. Client satisfaction > being right. A happy client refers. An argued-with client leaves.

Section 03 — Handover Execution

Go Deliver Your Work

Students with in-person meetings: go to the client now. Remote handovers: WhatsApp video call from class. Trainer accompanies the first 2 students.

Handover Execution Plan

  • In-person handovers: Go to client's business with deliverables on phone/laptop. Trainer accompanies first 2 students.
  • Remote handovers: WhatsApp video call from class. Trainer listens and coaches.
  • Students without clients: Work on proposals for remaining contacts OR assist classmates with their handovers (learn by observation).
📱

Payment Request Script

In-person: "Aapka invoice yeh raha. UPI se kar sakte hain abhi, ya bank transfer bhi chalega." Remote: Share invoice on WhatsApp with UPI ID/QR code. Have your Zoho invoice or Google Docs invoice ready to share instantly.

📋

If Client Says "Kal Bhejta Hoon"

That is okay. Do not pressure. Respond: "Ji bilkul, kal tak aa jaaye toh achha hoga kyunki main aapka next month ka plan banane waala hoon." This creates gentle urgency without being pushy.

The Retainer Upsell

One-time projects = hustle every month. Monthly retainers = predictable income. If the client is happy, pitch the retainer NOW while they are impressed.

Retainer Pitch Script

Click to copy: "[Name] ji, aapko yeh posts pasand aaye? Bahut achha! Ab sochiye -- agar har hafte 3 aise posts milte rahein, toh aapke business ki online presence kitni strong ho jayegi.
