SECTION 1🏢

Business Overview

Name, type, location, size, number of employees, current revenue range. Get the basics right — this shows you understand the business.

SECTION 2🚨

Current Challenges

Top 3 problems from your field research. Be specific: "Takes 2 hours daily to create test papers" is better than "wastes time."

SECTION 3🤖

AI Solutions

Specific AI tools AND actual prompts for each problem. Don't just say "use ChatGPT" — write the exact prompt and show the output.

SECTION 4📅

Implementation Steps

Week-by-week plan. Week 1: Set up tools. Week 2: Train staff. Week 3: Go live. Week 4: Review and optimize.

SECTION 5📈

Expected Results

Time saved per week, cost reduction, revenue potential. Use real numbers: "Saves 10 hours/week on paper creation = ₹5,000/month value."

SECTION 6💰

Investment Needed

₹0 for free tools, or minimal cost. This is the killer slide — "all these improvements for FREE" is extremely compelling.

Team Formation Strategy

Ideally pair: someone with business knowledge + someone with strong AI skills. Each team picks ONE business to work on from their field research. If someone doesn't have field research, pair them with someone who does.

Presentation Format (3 Minutes Per Team)

  • Business: What business, where, what they do (30 seconds)
  • Problem: Top 3 challenges from field research (30 seconds)
  • AI Solution: Specific tools and prompts for each problem (60 seconds)
  • Expected Impact: Time saved, cost saved, revenue potential (30 seconds)
  • Live Demo: Show one prompt running live (30 seconds)
