Section 03 — Negotiation

Negotiation Crash Course

Quick 10-minute refresher for students heading into closing conversations. These 4 techniques handle 90% of price negotiations.

TECHNIQUE 1💎

Add Value, Not Discount

Never lower your price first. Offer MORE value instead. "Price kam nahi hoga, but main aapke liye 2 extra reels add kar dunga first month mein." The client gets more. You keep your rate.

TECHNIQUE 2⏰

Create Urgency

"Yeh introductory pricing hai. Next month se ₹2,000 zyada hoga." Urgency works because people fear losing a deal more than they desire getting one. Use it honestly.

TECHNIQUE 3🎯

Start Small

"₹5,000 ka plan le lo. 1 mahina dekhte hain. Pasand aaye toh upgrade karenge." Getting ANY deal -- even ₹2,000 -- is more important than getting the perfect deal. A foot in the door.

TECHNIQUE 4🤝

The Final Close

"Toh shuru karte hain? Main kal se kaam start kar sakta hoon. Bas advance de dijiye." Direct, confident, professional. Ask for the business. Do not wait for them to offer.

Price Resistance Script

Click to copy: "Ji main samajhta hoon. Dekhiye, hamara sabse chhota plan ₹3,000/month hai. Ek din ka ₹100 bhi nahi banta. Isme aapko 12 professional posts milenge, Google listing, aur WhatsApp setup. Pehla mahina try kijiye -- pasand nahi aaya toh band kar denge. Koi commitment nahi. Shuru karein?"
🔑

The Golden Rule of Negotiation

Getting ANY deal -- even ₹2,000 -- is more important than getting the ₹10,000 deal. A small client who pays is infinitely more valuable than a big prospect who says 'sochke batata hoon' forever. Close the small deal. Deliver amazing work. Then upsell.

How to Call for Backup

Step 1: You are at a business, owner is interested but hesitant. Step 2: Say: "Ek minute, main apni team lead se baat karwa deta hoon." Step 3: Call trainer, briefly explain: "HOT lead at [business]. Owner interested in [package]. Need help closing." Step 4: Hand phone to owner or put on speaker. Trainer handles the rest.

Trainer's Movement Plan Today
