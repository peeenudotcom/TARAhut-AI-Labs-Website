Pricing Strategy & First Client

Golden rules for landing your first client. Realistic expectations, smart pricing, package strategy. "Pehla client mushkil hunda hai. Par pehle ton baad, har ik easy ho jaanda hai."

💰

4 Golden Rules for Your First Client

1. First 2-3 clients: FREE or very cheap (₹500-1,000). Build portfolio and testimonials.
2. After 3 clients: Charge market rate. You have proof now.
3. Always get a testimonial and permission to show the work.
4. Punjab pricing vs Global pricing — both are valid. Local: lower price, easier clients. Global: higher price, more competition.
