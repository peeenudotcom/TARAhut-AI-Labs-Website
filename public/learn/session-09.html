The Magic Explained Simply

जादू को आसानी से समझें

ਜਾਦੂ ਨੂੰ ਆਸਾਨੀ ਨਾਲ ਸਮਝੋ

Imagine taking a clear photo and slowly adding static noise until it becomes pure fuzz. Now imagine teaching an AI to reverse that process — to look at fuzz and intelligently remove noise based on your description. That's a diffusion model. It starts from random noise and gradually sculpts your image, guided by your text prompt.

जादू को आसानी से समझें

कल्पना करें कि एक साफ़ फ़ोटो में धीरे-धीरे शोर जोड़ रहे हैं जब तक वो पूरी तरह धुंधली न हो जाए। अब सोचें कि AI को इस प्रक्रिया को उलटना सिखाया गया है — शोर को देखकर आपके विवरण के आधार पर बुद्धिमानी से हटाना। यही diffusion model है। यह रैंडम शोर से शुरू होता है और धीरे-धीरे आपकी इमेज को तराशता है।

ਜਾਦੂ ਨੂੰ ਆਸਾਨੀ ਨਾਲ ਸਮਝੋ

ਸੋਚੋ ਕਿ ਇੱਕ ਸਾਫ਼ ਫ਼ੋਟੋ ਵਿੱਚ ਹੌਲੀ-ਹੌਲੀ ਸ਼ੋਰ ਮਿਲਾ ਰਹੇ ਹੋ ਜਦੋਂ ਤੱਕ ਉਹ ਪੂਰੀ ਤਰ੍ਹਾਂ ਧੁੰਦਲੀ ਨਾ ਹੋ ਜਾਵੇ। ਹੁਣ ਸੋਚੋ ਕਿ AI ਨੂੰ ਇਸ ਪ੍ਰਕਿਰਿਆ ਨੂੰ ਉਲਟਾਉਣਾ ਸਿਖਾਇਆ ਗਿਆ ਹੈ — ਤੁਹਾਡੇ ਵਰਣਨ ਦੇ ਅਧਾਰ ਤੇ ਸ਼ੋਰ ਹਟਾਉਣਾ। ਇਹੀ diffusion model ਹੈ। ਇਹ ਰੈਂਡਮ ਸ਼ੋਰ ਤੋਂ ਸ਼ੁਰੂ ਹੁੰਦਾ ਹੈ ਅਤੇ ਹੌਲੀ-ਹੌਲੀ ਤੁਹਾਡੀ ਇਮੇਜ ਘੜਦਾ ਹੈ।

Step 1: Your Prompt

You type: "A golden retriever wearing sunglasses on a beach at sunset, photorealistic." Every word matters. The AI will try to interpret each element.

Step 2: Text Encoding

A language model (similar to GPT) converts your text into a numerical "understanding" — a mathematical representation of what you mean. It knows "sunset" means warm colors, low light, horizon.

Step 3: Pure Noise

The AI starts with a completely random static image — like TV static. No information, just randomness. This is the starting canvas.

Step 4: Denoising (50 steps)

In about 50 small steps, the model removes noise while being "guided" by your text. Each step makes the image a little clearer, a little more like what you described. Like developing a photograph in a darkroom.

Step 5: Your Image

A final, coherent image emerges. It was never drawn — it was discovered by removing noise in a text-guided way. That's why AI images have such a unique, slightly surreal quality.

The Midjourney Prompt Formula

🔧

Structure: /imagine [Subject] + [Style] + [Details] + [Parameters]

Think of your prompt in 4 layers: What (subject) + How it looks (style) + Details (lighting, mood, camera) + Technical params (size, quality)

Basic Prompt
/imagine a cup of chai on a wooden table
Better Prompt (with style)
/imagine a steaming cup of masala chai on a rustic wooden table, warm morning light, food photography style, shallow depth of field
Professional Prompt (with parameters)
/imagine a steaming cup of masala chai on a rustic wooden table, warm golden morning light, food photography, shallow depth of field, Canon 85mm lens, bokeh background, cozy atmosphere --ar 4:5 --q 2 --v 6

Essential Parameters

ParameterWhat It DoesExample
--arAspect ratio of your image--ar 16:9 (landscape), --ar 4:5 (Instagram), --ar 1:1 (square)
--qQuality (higher = slower but better)--q 2 for maximum quality
--vMidjourney version to use--v 6 is latest (most realistic)

--noWhat to exclude from the image--no watermark, text, blurry
--seedReproduce same image with variations--seed 12345

After Your Image Generates

📺

U1-U4 (Upscale)

Upscale any of the 4 generated images to higher resolution. U1 = top-left, U2 = top-right, U3 = bottom-left, U4 = bottom-right.

🌎

V1-V4 (Variation)

Generate 4 new variations based on that image — same style and composition, but different. Use this to explore options.

🚀

Remix Mode

After upscaling, click "Vary (Region)" to edit just one part. Change the background, swap an element, keep the rest.
