AI Fails at Math — Until You Add 4 Words

AI Math में Fail — जब तक 4 शब्द न जोड़ो

AI Math ਵਿੱਚ Fail — ਜਦ ਤੱਕ 4 ਸ਼ਬਦ ਨਾ ਜੋੜੋ

"If a shirt costs ₹800 after a 20% discount, what was the original price?" GPT-4o gets this wrong without CoT. Add "Think step by step" and it gets it right. Those 4 words change everything. "4 shabd naal galat jawab sahi ho jaanda hai."

Without CoT: Wrong

"A shirt costs ₹800 after 20% discount. Original price?"
AI might answer: "₹960" (20% of 800 = 160, so 800+160=960).

✕ Wrong! If original is ₹960, 20% off = ₹768, not ₹800.

✕ Incorrect

With CoT: Correct

"Think step by step. A shirt costs ₹800 after 20% discount."
Step 1: 800 = original x (1 - 0.20)
Step 2: 800 = original x 0.80
Step 3: original = 800 / 0.80 = ₹1,000

✓ Correct

Why CoT Works

Normally, AI jumps from question to answer in one leap. CoT forces it to show its work, step by step. Each step checks the previous one. Like showing your work in math class. "Jive math class vich kaam dikhana — har step check hunda hai."

Section 02

Three Levels of CoT

From "just add 4 words" to providing complete reasoning structures. Each level gives you more control. "3 levels — har level zyada control dinda hai."

LEVEL 1💬

Zero-Shot CoT

Just add Think step by step or Let's work through this systematically. No examples needed. Easiest to use, surprisingly effective. Works for 70% of reasoning tasks. "Sirf 4 shabd jod do."

LEVEL 2📝

Manual CoT

You provide the reasoning structure: First, identify X. Then, calculate Y. Then, compare Z. Finally, conclude. You define the steps, AI follows them. More reliable for complex tasks. "Tusi steps define karo, AI follow kare."

LEVEL 3📚

Structured CoT

You provide a complete worked example WITH reasoning, then ask AI to follow the same pattern for a new problem. Most reliable, highest quality. "Pehle example dikhaao, phir nava problem do."

When CoT Matters Most

Use CoT for: Math, logic, multi-step analysis, decision-making, code debugging, legal reasoning, financial projections.
Skip CoT for: Simple facts ("What is the capital of France?"), translation, summarization, creative writing. "Jado sochna zaruri hai, CoT use karo. Jado simple kaam hai, zarurat nahi."

You now know 3 levels of CoT. Use Level 1 daily, Levels 2-3 for complex tasks.

Section 03

Tree-of-Thought — Multiple Paths

CoT is linear: one path of reasoning. Tree-of-Thought (ToT) explores MULTIPLE paths simultaneously, then picks the best one. Like a chess player thinking 3 moves ahead on different strategies. "CoT = ik raasta. ToT = 3 raaste explore karo, sabton vadiya chuno."
