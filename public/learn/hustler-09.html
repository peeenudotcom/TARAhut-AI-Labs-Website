What Makes Design "Commercial Quality"?

RULE 1🎨

Brand Consistency

Same colors, same fonts, same logo placement across ALL pieces. A business should be instantly recognizable from any design you create. "Ek brand ki pehchaan consistent honi chahiye."

RULE 2✏️

Less Text, More Impact

Maximum 20-30 words on any social media graphic. Use big headlines, minimal body text, and strong CTAs. "Zyada text = kam engagement. Chhota rakho, powerful rakho."

RULE 3🖼️

High-Quality Images

No pixelated photos. No watermarked stock images. Use Canva's built-in library or Unsplash. "Blurry photo = unprofessional impression."

RULE 4💲

Clear Call-to-Action

Every design must tell the viewer what to do next: Call now, Visit today, Order on WhatsApp, Book appointment. "Bina CTA ke design adhura hai."

💔

Amateur Design

Too many fonts, clashing colors, no CTA, tiny text, random images, no brand consistency. "Yeh WhatsApp forward jaisa lagta hai."

✕ Won't Sell
💖

Professional Design

2 fonts max, brand colors, clear CTA, proper hierarchy, high-quality images, consistent branding. "Yeh client dekhega toh turant haan bolega."

The 5-Minute Design Formula

A step-by-step system that turns any client brief into a polished design in exactly 5 minutes. Master this and you can produce 12 designs per hour.

💡

Why 5 Minutes?

If you spend 30 minutes per design, you make 2 designs/hour = ₹500-1000/hour. At 5 minutes per design, you make 12 designs/hour = ₹3000-6000/hour. Speed literally multiplies your income. Templates are your best friend.

💡

5 मिनट क्यों?

अगर आप एक डिज़ाइन में 30 मिनट लगाते हैं, तो 2 डिज़ाइन/घंटा = ₹500-1000/घंटा। 5 मिनट में, 12 डिज़ाइन/घंटा = ₹3000-6000/घंटा। स्पीड आपकी कमाई को मल्टीप्लाइ करती है।

💡

5 ਮਿੰਟ ਕਿਉਂ?

ਜੇ ਤੁਸੀਂ ਇੱਕ ਡਿਜ਼ਾਇਨ ਤੇ 30 ਮਿੰਟ ਲਾਉਂਦੇ ਹੋ, ਤਾਂ 2 ਡਿਜ਼ਾਇਨ/ਘੰਟਾ = ₹500-1000/ਘੰਟਾ। 5 ਮਿੰਟ ਵਿੱਚ, 12 ਡਿਜ਼ਾਇਨ/ਘੰਟਾ = ₹3000-6000/ਘੰਟਾ। ਸਪੀਡ ਤੁਹਾਡੀ ਕਮਾਈ ਨੂੰ ਮਲਟੀਪਲਾਈ ਕਰਦੀ ਹੈ।

STEP 1🔎

Pick Template (30 sec)

Search Canva for your design type. Pick the FIRST good template you see. Do not scroll for 10 minutes. Decision speed matters. "Pehla accha template lo, perfect template dhundhte mat raho."

STEP 2✏️

Change Text (1 min)

Replace all placeholder text with client content. Headlines first, then body text, then CTA. Keep the font the template uses — it was chosen by a professional designer.

STEP 3🎨

Swap Colors (1 min)

Apply the client's brand colors to the template. Background, accent, text colors. Maximum 3 colors in any design. Use Canva's "Brand Kit" feature if set up.

STEP 4🖼️

Replace Images (1 min)

Swap stock images with relevant ones from Canva's library. Or upload client photos. Make sure images are high quality and relevant to the business.

STEP 5📌

Add Logo + CTA (1 min)

Place the business logo (corner or bottom). Add phone number, WhatsApp, or website. Add the call-to-action button/text. Every design needs a "next step."

STEP 6💾

Review + Export (30 sec)

Quick proofread: no typos? Colors right? CTA visible? Export as PNG for social media, PDF for print. Done. "Typo = unprofessional. Hamesha check karo."

Section 04

Export Formats & Client Delivery

Knowing the right export format and having a professional delivery system separates amateurs from professionals. Clients pay more when the experience feels premium.

Export Format Guide

एक्सपोर्ट फॉर्मैट गाइड

ਇਕਸਪੋਰਟ ਫੌਰਮੈਟ ਗਾਇਡ

PNG🖼️

For Social Media

When: Instagram posts, Facebook posts, WhatsApp status, profile pictures.
Settings: PNG, highest quality.
Why: Transparent background support, sharp text, perfect for screens. "Social media ke liye PNG best hai."

JPG🌎

For Email & Web

When: Email attachments, website banners, blog images.
Settings: JPG, 80-100% quality.
Why: Smaller file size, faster loading. Good when transparency is not needed.

PDF Print🖨

For Printing

When: Business cards, posters, brochures, menus, flyers.
Settings: PDF Print, crop marks, bleed.
Why: 300 DPI, CMYK-ready, print shop compatible. "Print ke liye PDF Print format use karo."

MP4🎬

For Animated/Video

When: Animated Instagram posts, Stories with motion, video ads.
Settings: MP4, highest quality.
Why: Animated elements and transitions only show in video format.

📁

Professional File Naming Convention

Never send a file called "Design-final-v2-REAL-FINAL.png". Use this format:

[BusinessName]_[DesignType]_[Platform]_[Date].[ext]

Example: RajuSweets_DiwaliOffer_Instagram_2026-10-15.png

This looks professional and helps clients find files later. "Professional naming = professional impression."
