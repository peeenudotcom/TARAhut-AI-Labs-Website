Presentation Script Generator

Generate your presentation script with perfect timing:

📋 Write a 5-minute Demo Day presentation script for me. My project: [describe your capstone]. Problem I solved: [the pain point]. My AI solution: [what you built/demonstrated]. Results: [time saved, quality improved, etc.]. Structure it with exact timing markers: Introduction (30 seconds), Problem (1 minute), Solution with live demo cues (2 minutes), Results (1 minute), What's Next (30 seconds). Tone: confident, professional, with a touch of Punjabi warmth. Include a strong opening line and closing statement.

"Script tayyar hai ta confidence apne aap aunda hai."

⏱

The 5-Minute Rule

Your presentation is strictly 5 minutes. Not 6, not 7. Practice with a timer. If you're going over, cut content — don't speak faster. The audience includes business owners, parents, media, and faculty. Respect their time = earn their respect.

🎯

Strong Opening

"Sat Sri Akal! Main [name], te main [one sentence about what you built]." Hook them in 10 seconds. No "umm" or "actually."

📱

Live Demo

The most important part. Show, don't tell. Open ChatGPT/Canva/Zapier and demonstrate your solution working in real-time. Have a backup screenshot ready.

💪

Strong Closing

"8 hafte pehle main AI nahi jaanda/jaandi si. Aaj main [what you can do]. Shukriya, TARAhut AI Labs." End with confidence.

Practice Pairs — Right Now

Find a partner. Practice your 5-minute presentation. Time each other strictly. Give honest feedback: Was the demo clear? Did they finish on time? Was the opening strong? Practice again. Then once more.
