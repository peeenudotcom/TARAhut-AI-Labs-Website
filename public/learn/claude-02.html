Most people use Claude like a simple chat box — type, send, read. But Claude's interface has conversation management, model switching, file uploads, keyboard shortcuts, system-level instructions, and project organisation. Knowing these features means you get better outputs in less time. A Ludhiana freelancer who knows these shortcuts finishes work 40% faster than one who doesn't.

LEFT PANEL📁

Conversation Sidebar

The left sidebar shows all your past conversations grouped by date (Today, Yesterday, Previous 7 Days, etc.). Each conversation has a title auto-generated from your first message. You can rename conversations by clicking the title, delete conversations via the three-dot menu, and search through all past chats using the search bar at the top. Think of it as your AI filing cabinet.

TOP BAR🛠

Model Selector

At the top of any conversation, you'll see the model picker. Click it to switch between Sonnet, Opus, and Haiku. On the free tier, you get Sonnet. On Pro ($20/month), you unlock Opus for complex tasks and Haiku for speed. You can switch models mid-conversation — start with Sonnet for drafting, switch to Opus for final polish.

CENTRE💬

Chat Area

The main conversation area where messages flow. Your messages appear on the right, Claude's on the left. Claude's responses include a copy button (one click copies the entire response), a retry button (regenerates the response with slight variation), and thumbs up/down for feedback. Long responses have a scroll indicator.

You now know the three core zones of Claude's interface: sidebar, top bar, and chat area.अब आप Claude इंटरफ़ेस के तीन मुख्य ज़ोन जानते हैं: साइडबार, टॉप बार, और चैट एरिया।ਹੁਣ ਤੁਸੀਂ Claude ਇੰਟਰਫੇਸ ਦੇ ਤਿੰਨ ਮੁੱਖ ਜ਼ੋਨ ਜਾਣਦੇ ਹੋ: ਸਾਈਡਬਾਰ, ਟੌਪ ਬਾਰ, ਅਤੇ ਚੈਟ ਏਰੀਆ।

The Input Area — More Powerful Than You Think

The message input box at the bottom is not just a text field. It supports file attachments (click the paperclip or drag-and-drop), multi-line input (press Shift+Enter for new lines), and long-form pasting (you can paste entire documents directly). There's also a microphone icon for voice input on supported browsers. The send button turns into a stop button while Claude is generating, letting you halt a response mid-stream.

Settings Tab

Set your display name (this appears in conversations). Upload a profile picture. Add Custom Instructions — a system-level message that applies to ALL new conversations. This is where you tell Claude about yourself once, so you don't repeat it every time.

Settings Tab

Switch between Light mode, Dark mode, and System (follows your OS). Dark mode reduces eye strain during late-night work. Choose your preferred response language and set Claude's default response length (concise, normal, or detailed).

Settings Tab

Connect Claude to Google Drive, Notion, and other services. This lets Claude read documents directly from your cloud storage without manual uploading. Huge time saver for professionals who keep documents in Google Drive.

What is a Context Window?Context Window क्या है?Context Window ਕੀ ਹੈ?

The context window is Claude's working memory — everything it can "see" at once during a conversation. 200,000 tokens equals roughly 150,000 words. An average novel is 80,000 words. Claude can hold almost two full novels in its memory simultaneously. You can paste an entire contract, business plan, or research paper and Claude will read and understand ALL of it — not just skim the beginning like some other AI tools.

Why Shortcuts MatterShortcuts ਕਿਉਂ ਜ਼ਰੂਰੀ ਹਨShortcuts ਕਿਉਂ ਜ਼ਰੂਰੀ ਹਨ

Each shortcut saves 2-5 seconds. If you use Claude 50 times a day, that's 4-8 minutes saved daily, or roughly 30-40 hours per year. For a Ludhiana professional billing at ₹2,000/hour, that's ₹60,000-80,000 worth of recovered time annually. Shortcuts aren't just convenient — they're profitable.

Shortcut 1

⚡ Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + O

New Conversation. Instantly starts a fresh chat without touching the sidebar. The fastest way to start a new topic.

Shortcut 2

⚡ Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + ;

Toggle Sidebar. Hides or shows the conversation sidebar. Hide it for distraction-free, full-width writing.

Shortcut 3

⚡ Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + C

Copy Last Response. Copies Claude's most recent response to clipboard instantly. No scrolling needed.

Shortcut 4

⚡ Shift + Enter

New Line. Adds a line break without sending your message. Essential for multi-paragraph prompts.

Shortcut 5

⚡ Ctrl/Cmd + /

Show All Shortcuts. Opens the full keyboard shortcuts reference panel. The shortcut to find shortcuts.

Shortcut 6

⚡ Escape

Stop Generation. Halts Claude mid-response. Use when Claude is heading in the wrong direction.

Shortcut 7

⚡ Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Del

Delete Conversation. Removes the current conversation permanently. Keeps your sidebar clean.
