Chain-of-Thought Prompting

Make AI think step by step before giving you the final answer. This dramatically improves accuracy on complex problems, reasoning tasks, and multi-step decisions.

🧠

What is Chain-of-Thought (CoT)?

Chain-of-Thought prompting is a technique where you instruct AI to show its reasoning process step by step before arriving at a conclusion. Research from Google Brain shows that CoT prompting can improve AI accuracy on complex problems by 30-60%. The magic phrase: "Think step by step."

❌ Without Chain-of-Thought
Should I start a clothing shop or a digital marketing agency?
AI gives a generic comparison with surface-level pros/cons. No reasoning. No personalisation. Hard to trust.
✅ With Chain-of-Thought
I am deciding between opening a clothing shop or a digital marketing agency in Kotkapura. Think through this step by step: First analyse the local market. Then consider my situation (22 years old, ₹50K savings, 6 months free time, no business experience). Then weigh the risks. Finally give me your recommendation with clear reasoning.
AI walks through each factor methodically. The recommendation is grounded in visible reasoning you can evaluate and question.

The 4 CoT Trigger Phrases — Copy Any of These

Phrase 1: "Think step by step"

The classic CoT trigger. Works in almost every situation. Forces AI to reason before concluding.

Analyse the pros and cons of switching from a job to freelancing. Think step by step. Then give me your conclusion.

Phrase 2: "Walk me through your reasoning"

Useful when you want to see the logic, not just the answer — especially for business decisions or strategy.

Should I invest my savings in a fixed deposit or mutual fund? Walk me through your reasoning considering I am 24 years old with 2 years of expenses saved.

Phrase 3: "First think, then answer"

Explicitly separates the thinking phase from the answering phase. Great for complex analysis.

First think through all the factors involved in starting a food delivery service in a Tier-3 city. Then give me your analysis in 3 clear sections: Opportunities, Challenges, First Steps.

Phrase 4: "Break this down for me before answering"

Useful when you want AI to decompose a complex topic into understandable parts before synthesising.

Break down what 'machine learning' really means before explaining how I could use it in my small business without coding.
💡

When to Use Chain-of-Thought

CoT is most powerful for: Business decisions (should I do X or Y?), Problem solving (why is my sales not growing?), Planning (how do I reach goal Z?), Analysis (what are the real risks here?), and Math / logic problems. For simple tasks (write a caption, translate this), CoT is unnecessary overhead.

You just learned a technique that researchers formally published in academic AI papers. And you can use it right now.

60-Second Action

Think of one real decision you're currently facing. Open ChatGPT and ask: "[Your decision question]. Think step by step: first list all the relevant factors, then weigh them, then give me your recommendation." Compare this to how you'd normally ask the question.

Section 02

Few-Shot Prompting

Give AI 2-3 examples of what you want before asking it to produce. This is how you teach AI your specific style, tone, and format — permanently changing how it responds to you.

🎯

The Core Idea: Show, Don't Just Tell

Instead of describing what you want (which can be misinterpreted), you show AI examples of the exact pattern you want. Then ask it to create more like those. AI learns your pattern from the examples and replicates it precisely. This is how you clone your own writing style.

Before/After Comparisons — Same Goal, Different Approach

❌ Zero-Shot (No Examples)
Write 3 Instagram captions for a clothing shop. Make them engaging and use Hinglish.
AI gives generic captions in whatever "Hinglish" it decides. May not match your brand voice at all.
✅ Few-Shot (With Examples)
Write 3 more Instagram captions in the EXACT same style as these examples I wrote:

Example 1: "Naya collection aa gaya hai! 🌸 Desi vibes with a modern twist. Kotkapura walo, aao check karo!"

Example 2: "Eid special suits ready hain! 💛 Sabse best quality, sabse best price — guarantee se."

Now write 3 more for our summer collection.
AI perfectly replicates your Hinglish style, your emoji usage, your sentence structure, and your brand voice.
❌ Zero-Shot
Classify these customer reviews as positive, negative, or neutral: "Great product!" / "Didn't work for me." / "It's okay I guess."
May work, but AI might use different labels or add explanations you don't need.
✅ Few-Shot
Classify reviews exactly like these examples:
"Amazing quality!" → POSITIVE
"Broke after 2 days." → NEGATIVE
"Decent but nothing special." → NEUTRAL

Now classify: "Great product!" / "Didn't work for me." / "It's okay I guess."
AI outputs exactly the format you showed — no extra explanation, perfect consistency.
⚡

The Few-Shot Formula

1) Show 2-3 examples of the pattern/style/format you want
2) Label them clearly as "Example 1", "Example 2" etc.
3) Say "Now do the same for: [your new input]"

The more precise your examples, the more precisely AI replicates the pattern. This works for writing styles, data formats, classification tasks, and creative work.

Few-Shot prompting is used by professional AI developers daily. You now have the same technique.
You're no longer writing prompts — you're teaching AI to work in your exact style. That's a different level entirely.

Try it Now: Clone Your Style

Find 2 pieces of writing you've done that you're proud of (WhatsApp messages, social posts, anything). Copy them as Few-Shot examples into ChatGPT and ask it to "write 3 more in the exact same style." The result will surprise you.

System Prompts & Personas

Create permanent AI personalities that behave exactly how you need — every conversation, every time. This is how businesses build custom AI assistants.

🤖

What is a System Prompt?

A system prompt is a set of instructions given to AI before the conversation starts — defining its persona, rules, tone, knowledge, and constraints. Businesses use system prompts to build custom AI assistants (like customer service bots). In ChatGPT, you can set one in Settings → Personalization → Custom Instructions. In Claude, you put it at the very start of your first message.

6 Ready-to-Use Personas — Click to Copy

Always use simple language, local Indian examples (cricket, festivals, food, Bollywood), and end every explanation with a simple analogy. If the student seems confused, offer to explain differently.
Use for: studying any subject, understanding complex topics, preparing for exams

⚙️

How to Set a System Prompt in ChatGPT

Option 1 — Custom Instructions: Settings → Personalization → Custom Instructions → "How would you like ChatGPT to respond?" → Paste your system prompt here. Now every new conversation starts with that persona.

Option 2 — GPT Builder: Create → Explore GPTs → Create a GPT → Add your system prompt. You now have a dedicated custom AI assistant you can reuse.

Building Your Prompt Library

A Prompt Library is a personal collection of tested, effective prompts organised by category. Once built, it saves you hours every week and grows more valuable over time.

📚

Why a Prompt Library is Your Most Valuable AI Asset

Every time you craft a great prompt that produces excellent results — save it. Within 3 months of active AI use, you'll have a library of 50+ tested prompts that consistently deliver. This library is your competitive advantage. No one else has your exact collection of prompts tuned to your business, your audience, and your goals.

The Template: How to Organise Every Prompt

CategoryPrompt (RCTF Format)When to UseBest Tool
📱 Social Media Act as a social media expert for Indian SMEs. I run [BUSINESS] in [CITY]. Write 5 Instagram captions for [OCCASION/PRODUCT]. Format: Caption | Hashtags | Posting time. Before every product launch, festival, or campaign ChatGPT
📧 Email Outreach Act as a professional business communication expert. I am a [ROLE] reaching out to [TARGET]. Write a cold email to [GOAL]. Under 200 words, end with CTA to [ACTION]. Reaching new clients, partners, or opportunities Claude
🔍 Research Act as a research analyst. I need to understand [TOPIC] for my [PURPOSE]. Provide: 5 key facts, 3 surprising insights, and 3 actionable implications. Cite confidence level for each point. Before making any major business or career decision Perplexity
📝 Content Creation Act as a content strategist. I need a [CONTENT TYPE] about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE]. The tone should be [TONE]. Format as [FORMAT]. Word count: under [N] words. Blogs, newsletters, scripts, descriptions, whitepapers ChatGPT / Claude
💡 Brainstorming Act as a creative business consultant. My challenge: [PROBLEM]. Think step by step. Generate 10 unconventional ideas to solve this. Rank by effort vs impact. Suggest the top 3 to try first. When stuck on any problem, big or small ChatGPT
🎨 Image Prompts [Subject], [Style], [Mood/Lighting], [Camera angle], [Color palette], [Extra details]. Photorealistic / Illustration / Oil painting. Generating visuals for content, products, or designs Midjourney / Ideogram

Where to Store Your Prompt Library

Notion: Best for rich formatting, tables, tags, and searchability. Free plan is enough.
Google Docs / Sheets: Simple, accessible on any device, easy to share.
Notes App: Quick and always available on your phone.
Obsidian: For power users who want a knowledge graph of interconnected prompts.

The best system is the one you'll actually use consistently. Start simple.

Think about this: A professional chef doesn't memorise every recipe from scratch every day. They build a personal recipe book over years. A good prompt library does the same thing for your AI work. Your future self will thank your present self for building this now.
