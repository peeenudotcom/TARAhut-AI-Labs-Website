What Businesses Actually Want in Video

MOST REQUESTED🎬

Business Introduction

60-second slideshow or avatar video. Who we are, what we do, why choose us. Every business needs this as their anchor content. "Yeh har business ko chahiye."

HIGH DEMAND🍽

Product / Menu Showcase

30-second visual tour of products or menu items. Great for restaurants, salons, retail. Close-up photos + voiceover description + pricing. "Product dikhao, sale badhao."

SEASONAL🎉

Festival Greetings

15-30 second branded greetings. Diwali, Baisakhi, Eid, Christmas, New Year. Simple, emotional, shareable. Clients love sharing these on WhatsApp Status. "Festival pe sabko video chahiye."

PREMIUM⭐

Customer Testimonials

Real customer stories with AI voiceover or text overlays. Social proof sells. Can be avatar-based or slideshow format. Higher value: ₹3000-5000 each.

EDUCATIONAL📚

How-To / Tips Content

"5 tips for healthy teeth" for a dental clinic. "How to style your kurta" for a boutique. Positions the business as an expert. Great for Instagram Reels.

💡

Speed Production Tips

1. Batch scripts first: Write ALL scripts in one ChatGPT session before producing any video.
2. Template reuse: Same Canva template, different content = consistent branding.
3. Voice consistency: Same ElevenLabs voice for all videos of one client.
4. CapCut templates: Save your first video as a template for uniform style.

Video Specs By Platform

Wrong dimensions = unprofessional. Know the exact specs for every platform your clients use.

INSTAGRAM REEL📷

9:16 Vertical

Size: 1080 x 1920 pixels
Duration: 30-60 seconds ideal
Audio: Required (music + voiceover)
Captions: Essential (80% watch muted)
Best for: Tips, behind-scenes, product showcases

YOUTUBE SHORT📺

9:16 Vertical

Size: 1080 x 1920 pixels
Duration: Up to 60 seconds
Audio: Voiceover works great
Captions: Auto-generated available
Best for: Business intros, educational tips

WHATSAPP STATUS💬

9:16 Vertical

Size: 1080 x 1920 pixels
Duration: Max 30 seconds
Audio: Optional (many watch on silent)
Captions: Critical (muted viewing)
Best for: Offers, greetings, quick announcements

FACEBOOK POST💻

1:1 or 16:9

Size: 1080x1080 (square) or 1920x1080 (landscape)
Duration: 60-120 seconds
Audio: With captions backup
Best for: Detailed business introductions, testimonials
