What is AI? 🧠

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. That's a fancy way of saying — a super-smart computer helper that learned from millions of books, websites, and pictures!

🐶 Think of it like training a puppy!

When you teach a puppy "sit" — you show it, reward it, and practice again and again. AI works the same way! People showed AI billions of examples until it got really good at helping. Just like your puppy learned tricks, AI learned how to talk, write, and create!
