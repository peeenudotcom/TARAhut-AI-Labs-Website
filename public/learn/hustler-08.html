Professional Flyer Design

प्रोफेशनल फ्लायर डिज़ाइन

ਪ੍ਰੋਫੈਸ਼ਨਲ ਫਲਾਇਰ ਡਿਜ਼ਾਈਨ

Flyers are the #1 print marketing tool for local businesses. Grand openings, sale announcements, event promotions, coaching center admissions — all need flyers. You'll create print-ready flyers in 5 minutes.

📄

Why Flyers Still Work in 2026

Digital is king, but local businesses still need physical flyers. Grand opening flyers distributed in mohallas. Sale flyers placed on car windshields. Coaching center flyers at school gates. Event flyers at Gurudwaras and temples. Digital design, physical distribution. You design both digital AND print versions from the same Canva file.

"Kal Instagram posts banaye. Aaj real marketing materials. Jab client bolega 'mujhe 500 flyers print karwane hain grand opening ke liye' — tum 5 minute mein design de doge. Printing wala alag. Design tumhara. INR 1,500-3,000 per flyer design."
🏪Flyer Type

Grand Opening Flyer

New shop, restaurant, salon opening. Needs: business name, opening date, address, inaugural offers, map/directions. High-energy design with bold colors and "GRAND OPENING" text.

🎁Flyer Type

Sale / Offer Flyer

Seasonal sale, Diwali offers, clearance sale. Needs: discount percentage, products on sale, validity dates, T&C. Red/yellow urgency colors. "LIMITED TIME" messaging.

🎓Flyer Type

Coaching / Admissions

Tuition centers, coaching classes, schools. Needs: subjects, batch timings, fees, faculty, previous results, contact. Professional, trust-building design.

🎈Flyer Type

Event / Party Flyer

Weddings, kitty parties, jagran, community events. Needs: event name, date, time, venue, entry details, entertainment lineup. Festive, vibrant design.

💉Flyer Type

Service Flyer

Plumbers, electricians, AC repair, pest control. Needs: services list, pricing, phone number, service area. Clean, professional, phone-number-focused.

🏠Flyer Type

Real Estate Flyer

Property dealers, new plots, flats for sale/rent. Needs: property photos, price, area, amenities, location map. Premium design with property images.

❌ Common Flyer Mistakes

  • Too much text crammed in small space
  • Low-resolution images (blurry when printed)
  • No clear hierarchy — everything looks same importance
  • Missing phone number or address
  • Wrong color mode (RGB for print instead of CMYK)
  • No bleed area for printing

✅ Professional Flyer Rules

  • Clear hierarchy: headline > subhead > details > CTA
  • Large phone number (biggest text after headline)
  • High-resolution images (300 DPI for print)
  • Brand colors consistently applied
  • White space around important elements
  • Download as "PDF Print" for print shops

Business Card Pro Tips

Tip 1: QR code on back = modern touch. Link to Google Maps, WhatsApp, or website.
Tip 2: Matte vs Glossy: suggest matte for professionals (doctors, lawyers), glossy for creative (photographers, designers).
Tip 3: Always design at 300 DPI. Canva's "PDF Print" handles this automatically.
Tip 4: Print a test copy before bulk order. Colors look different on screen vs paper.
Tip 5: Offer digital business card too (PNG for WhatsApp sharing). 2-in-1 value.
