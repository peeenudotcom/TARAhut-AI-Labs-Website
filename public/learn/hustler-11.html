CapCut AI — Video Editing

CapCut is your free, powerful video editor. Import slides, add voiceover, sync timing, add text overlays and music. No experience needed.

CapCut Essential Skills

SKILL 1📤

Import Media

Import your Canva slides (exported as individual PNG images) and your ElevenLabs voiceover (MP3). Drag them onto the timeline. Slides go on the video track, voiceover on the audio track.

SKILL 2⏰

Sync Timing

Match each slide duration to the voiceover timing. Listen to the voiceover, mark when each topic changes, and set slide transitions at those points. This is the most important editing skill.

SKILL 3💬

Text Overlays

Add key words as text overlays on slides. Not the full script — just keywords and CTAs. Use CapCut's AI auto-captions for subtitles. "Subtitles add karo — 80% log mute mein dekhte hain."

SKILL 4🎵

Background Music

Add subtle background music from CapCut's free library. Keep volume at 10-20% of the voiceover. Music sets the mood but should never overpower the voice.

SKILL 5🎬

Transitions & Effects

Add smooth transitions between slides. Fade, dissolve, or slide. Keep it professional — no flashy or distracting transitions. Simple = professional. "Simple transitions = professional look."

SKILL 6💾

Export Settings

Export at 1080p minimum. Choose 9:16 for Reels/Shorts, 16:9 for YouTube, 1:1 for feed posts. Match platform requirements. "Sahi size mein export karo."

💡

CapCut AI Features to Use

Auto-Captions: Generates subtitles from audio automatically. Essential — 80% of social media videos are watched without sound.
Background Remover: Remove backgrounds from clips.
AI Text-to-Speech: CapCut has its own TTS if ElevenLabs credits run out.
Smart Resize: Convert 16:9 to 9:16 automatically.
