Google My Business — Your Free Storefront

Google My Business — आपका मुफ्त स्टोरफ्रंट

Google My Business — ਤੁਹਾਡਾ ਮੁਫ਼ਤ ਸਟੌਰਫ਼ਰੰਟ

When someone searches "best salon near me" or "AC repair Kotkapura," Google My Business decides who shows up FIRST. 92% of local searches happen on Google. If you're not on GMB, you don't exist for your customers. "Agar tusi Google te nahi ho, taan customers lyi tusi ho hi nahi."

💡

Why GMB Is Non-Negotiable

Google My Business is 100% free and gives you: a listing on Google Maps, appearance in "near me" searches, a place for customer reviews, direct messaging, post updates, and call tracking. A well-optimized GMB listing is worth more than a ₹50,000/month website. "Ih FREE hai par value lakhan di hai."

Step-by-Step GMB Setup

Step 1💻

Create Your Profile

Go to business.google.com. Sign in with a Google account. Enter your exact business name — no keyword stuffing. Select the right category (you get 1 primary + 9 additional). Choose your service area or physical location. "Business da naam bilkul sahi likho — koyi extra keyword na paao."

Step 2📍

Verify Your Business

Google sends a postcard with a PIN to your address (5-14 days). Some businesses can verify by phone, email, or video. Never skip verification — unverified listings don't rank. While waiting, fill out every single field in your profile. "Verification ton bina listing rank nahi karegi."

Step 3📸

Add Photos & Details

Upload minimum 10 photos: storefront, interior, products, team, behind-the-scenes. Listings with 100+ photos get 520% more calls than those with none. Add business hours, phone number, website, services list with prices, and attributes. "Jitni zyada photos, otni zyada calls."

Step 4💬

Write Your Description

750 characters to tell your story. Use AI to write it: "Write a Google My Business description for a [business type] in [city]. Include services, years of experience, and what makes us different. Keep it under 750 characters." Include local keywords naturally. "AI ton description likhwao — par apni language vich customize karo."

Review Strategy: The Growth Engine

Strategy⭐

How to Get Reviews

Ask at the moment of delight. Customer just complimented your work? "Thank you ji! Would you mind leaving us a Google review? It really helps small businesses like ours." Send the direct review link via WhatsApp. Create a QR code for the counter. Offer no incentives (against Google policy). "Jado customer khush hove, osi waqt review mango."

Critical💬

Responding to Reviews

Reply to EVERY review within 24 hours. Positive: "Thank you [Name]! We're glad you enjoyed [specific thing]. See you again soon!" Negative: "We're sorry about your experience, [Name]. We'd love to make it right. Please call us at [number]." Use AI to draft responses. Never argue publicly.

Pro Tip🔥

Review Velocity

Google rewards consistency. 5 reviews per week for 6 months beats 100 reviews in one week. Set a target: ask 1-2 customers per day. Track in a spreadsheet. Businesses with 50+ reviews and 4.5+ rating dominate the local pack. "Har roz 1-2 reviews mango, 6 mahine vich tusi top te hovoge."

Local SEO — Ranking for "Near Me"

Local SEO — "Near Me" में रैंकिंग

Local SEO — "Near Me" ਵਿੱਚ ਰੈਂਕਿੰਗ

"Near me" searches have grown 500% in 5 years. When someone types "best bakery near me" or "plumber in Faridkot," Google uses 3 factors to decide who shows up: Relevance, Distance, and Prominence. You can control 2 of these 3. "Tuhade shehar vich sabton pehle dikhan da formula sikhde haan."

🎯
Relevance
How well your listing matches the search query. Use right categories & keywords.
📍
Distance
How far your business is from the searcher. You can't control this.
⭐
Prominence
How well-known your business is. Reviews, citations, website quality.

The Local SEO Playbook

NAP Consistency📝

Name, Address, Phone

Your NAP must be IDENTICAL everywhere: Google, Facebook, Instagram, JustDial, Sulekha, your website, Yellow Pages. Even small differences hurt: "Road" vs "Rd" or "Kotkapura" vs "Kot Kapura." Create a master document with your exact NAP. "Har jagah ik hi naam, pata, te phone number hona chahida hai."

Local Citations🔗

Directory Listings

List your business on: JustDial, Sulekha, IndiaMart, Google Maps, Facebook, Bing Places, Apple Maps, Yelp India. Each citation is a "vote" telling Google you're a real business. Target 30+ citations. Use the same NAP everywhere. "Jitni zyada jagah listed, otna zyada Google trust karega."

On-Page SEO💻

Website Optimization

If you have a website, add location-specific pages: "Best AC Repair in Kotkapura," "Salon Services in Faridkot." Include: city name in title tag, H1, meta description, and content. Add Google Map embed. Add schema markup for local business. "Website te shehar da naam har jagah hona chahida hai."

Content Strategy📰

Local Content

Create content about local events, news, and community. "Best restaurants for Diwali dinner in Bathinda" or "Monsoon AC maintenance tips for Moga homes." Google loves locally relevant content. Blog weekly about topics your customers search for. "Local content = local ranking."

🚫

Wrong: Generic SEO

"Best salon in India" — too broad, too competitive. "Hair services" — no location. "Contact us for more info" — no keywords. Generic content that could be from anywhere.

✕ Won't Rank Locally
✅

Right: Hyper-Local SEO

"Best bridal makeup salon in Kotkapura" — specific, local, targeted. "AC repair service near Faridkot bus stand" — micro-local. "Wedding photographer Bathinda starting ₹15,000" — with pricing.

✓ Ranks in Top 3
💡

The Google Local Pack

The "Local Pack" is the map with 3 business listings that appears at the top of local searches. Being in the Local Pack = 33% of all clicks. The formula: complete GMB profile + consistent NAP + 50+ reviews + local content + mobile-friendly website. Most local businesses do NONE of these things. Do all 5 and you'll dominate. "Top 3 vich aana hai taan 5 cheezaan karo."

WhatsApp Business — Your Sales Machine

WhatsApp Business — आपका सेल्स मशीन

WhatsApp Business — ਤੁਹਾਡਾ ਸੇਲਜ਼ ਮਸ਼ੀਨ

India has 500M+ WhatsApp users. Your customers are already ON WhatsApp. WhatsApp Business is free, powerful, and has a 98% open rate (vs 20% for email). Every local business should have a WhatsApp Business account. "Tuhade customers pehle ton hi WhatsApp te ne — bas tusi professionally use nahi kar rahe."

WhatsApp Business Setup

Setup📱

Download & Configure

Download WhatsApp Business (separate app from regular WhatsApp). Set up with your business phone number. Add: business name, category, description, address, email, website, business hours. Upload a professional profile photo (your logo, not your face). "Pehle app download karo, fer sab details bharo."

Catalog🛒

Product Catalog

Add your products/services with photos, descriptions, and prices. This turns WhatsApp into a mini-website. Customers can browse your catalog directly in the chat. Add at least 10 items. Include ₹ prices (builds trust). Use high-quality photos. "Catalog = tuhadi free website WhatsApp vich."

Auto-Reply🤖

Automated Messages

Set up: Greeting message (auto-sent to first-time contacts), Away message (when you're unavailable), Quick replies (saved responses for FAQs). Example greeting: "Sat Sri Akal ji! Welcome to [Business]. How can we help you today? Reply 1 for Services, 2 for Prices, 3 for Location." "Har nayi inquiry da automatic jawab."

Labels🏷

Organize Contacts

Use labels to categorize contacts: "New Lead," "Pending Payment," "Repeat Customer," "VIP." Color-code them. This is your free CRM inside WhatsApp. Filter and message specific groups. Track your sales pipeline without any extra software. "WhatsApp = free CRM."

WhatsApp Broadcast Lists vs Groups

Broadcast Lists send a message to multiple people individually (they see it as a personal message). Groups show all members. For marketing: ALWAYS use Broadcast Lists. They feel personal, don't annoy people, and have much higher engagement. Limit: 256 contacts per list. Create multiple lists by category. "Broadcast = personal feel, Group = spam feel."

Keyword Research with Ubersuggest & AI

Ubersuggest & AI से Keyword Research

Ubersuggest & AI ਨਾਲ Keyword Research

Keyword research answers ONE question: "What words do my customers type into Google when they need my service?" If you know the exact words, you can appear in front of them at the exact moment they need you. "Customers Google vich ki type karde ne — oh words tumhare golden keywords ne."

🔍 Ubersuggest (Free)

Neil Patel's free keyword tool. Enter any keyword and get: search volume, SEO difficulty, CPC, and related keywords. Free version gives 3 searches/day. Perfect for beginners. Go to neilpatel.com/ubersuggest.

💡 Google Autocomplete

Type your service in Google and DON'T press Enter. The suggestions = what people actually search. "AC repair" shows "AC repair near me," "AC repair cost," "AC repair at home." These are your keywords. Free and always up to date.

💬 Google "People Also Ask"

Search your service on Google. The "People Also Ask" box shows related questions. "How much does AC repair cost?" "Is AC repair covered by warranty?" Each question = a blog post or FAQ answer on your website.

🤖 AI Keyword Research

Ask ChatGPT/Claude: "What would someone search on Google when they need [your service] in [your city]?" AI generates 50+ keyword ideas in seconds, including long-tail variations you'd never think of.

Keyword Categories for Local Business

Service Keywords🔧

What You Offer

"AC repair Kotkapura," "bridal makeup Faridkot," "best gym Bathinda," "plumber near me." These are high-intent transactional keywords — people searching these are ready to buy. Target these FIRST.

Problem Keywords😕

What Customers Suffer

"AC not cooling," "water leaking from AC," "hair falling out treatment," "back pain relief." People search their problems, not your services. Match your content to their pain. "Customers apni problem search karde ne, tuhadi service nahi."

Comparison Keywords⚖

Decision Stage

"AC repair vs replace," "salon A vs salon B [city]," "gym price comparison Ludhiana." These searchers are comparing options before buying. Create comparison content to capture them. "Jado comparison search karde ne, taan buying decision le rahe hunde ne."

Google Analytics & Search Console Basics

Google Analytics & Search Console की बेसिक्स

Google Analytics & Search Console ਦੀਆਂ ਬੇਸਿਕਸ

"Jo measure nahi hunda, oh improve nahi ho sakda." You need to know: How many people visit your site? Where do they come from? What do they search? Which pages work? Google gives you two FREE tools for this. Let's set them up.

📊

Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

Tracks website visitors: who they are, where they come from, what they do on your site, how long they stay, what they click. The "CCTV camera" for your website.

Website Traffic
🔎

Google Search Console

Shows how Google sees your site: what keywords people search, your rankings, click-through rates, technical issues. The "report card" from Google itself.

Search Performance

Google Analytics 4: What to Track

Metric 1👥

Users & Sessions

How many unique people visit your site (users) and how many times they visit (sessions). Track daily, weekly, monthly trends. A growing trend = your marketing is working. Target: 10% growth month-over-month.

Metric 2📍

Traffic Sources

Where visitors come from: Organic Search (Google), Direct (typed URL), Social (Facebook/Instagram), Referral (other websites), Paid (ads). Know which channel brings the most customers. Double down on what works.

Metric 3⏱

Engagement

Avg. engagement time: how long people actually interact with your site. Bounce rate: % who leave without doing anything. Good engagement = 2+ minutes. High bounce = your page doesn't match what they searched for.

Metric 4🎯

Conversions

Set up conversion tracking for: form submissions, phone calls, WhatsApp clicks, appointment bookings. This tells you not just who visits but who becomes a customer. Without conversion tracking, you're flying blind. "Bina conversion tracking, tusi andhe ho."

Search Console: Key Reports

Report🔍

Search Performance

Shows: Queries (what people searched), Impressions (how many times you appeared), Clicks (how many clicked), CTR (click-through rate), Position (your average ranking). Filter by date, page, country, device. This is GOLD for keyword strategy.

Report🚧

Coverage / Indexing

Shows which pages Google has indexed (can find in search) and which have errors. Fix errors ASAP: 404 pages, redirect issues, crawl blocked pages. If Google can't find your page, customers can't either.

Setup Exercise: Connect Your Tools

Do these right now:

  • Google Analytics: Go to analytics.google.com → Create Account → Add your website → Install tracking code
  • Search Console: Go to search.google.com/search-console → Add Property → Verify ownership
  • Connect Search Console to Analytics (Settings → Product Links)
  • Connect Search Console to your GMB listing
⚠️

Data Takes Time

Google Analytics and Search Console need 2-4 weeks to collect meaningful data. Don't make decisions based on 3 days of data. Set up now, check weekly, make changes monthly. "Sabr rakho — data ikkattha hone do, fer decisions lo."
