Deepfake Detective 🔍

Can you tell what's real and what's AI-generated? Study each case carefully — then make your call. Your score is tracked!

⚠️

What Is a Deepfake?

A deepfake is a fake video, image, or audio created by AI that looks or sounds real. The name comes from "deep learning" + "fake". They can make people appear to say or do things they never did — and they're getting harder to detect.

🕵️ Examine each scenario — Real or AI-Generated?

🔎

Real Deepfake Warning Signs

Blurry edges around face and hair • Unnatural blinking (too fast or too slow) • Weird lighting on face vs background • Mouth sync doesn't match words perfectly • Glitchy ears/teeth in photos • Too perfect skin with no pores or texture • Background details look "dreamlike"

The AI Courtroom 🏛️

Big questions need good arguments. Read BOTH sides of each debate — then decide where YOU stand!

⚖️

How to Debate Well

Good debaters don't just shout opinions — they give reasons and evidence. Even if you disagree, try to understand the other side. The best thinkers can argue BOTH sides convincingly.

🤖 "Should AI do your homework?"

✅ For

AI accelerates learning, teaches research skills, and mirrors real-world work where professionals use every tool available. The skill is knowing how to use AI, not memorising answers.

❌ Against

Doing homework builds critical thinking and independent problem-solving. If AI does it all, you miss the struggle that creates real understanding. Submitting AI work as your own is dishonest.

🎯 MIDDLE GROUND: Use AI to understand, not to replace your effort.

👩‍🏫 "Should AI replace teachers?"

✅ For

AI tutors are available 24/7, never lose patience, can personalise to each student's level, and can teach any subject at any pace. In underserved areas, AI could democratise great education.

❌ Against

Teachers provide mentorship, emotional support, and human connection that AI cannot replicate. Teaching is not just information transfer — it's inspiration, belonging, and human relationship.

🎯 REALITY: AI assists great teachers, not replaces them.

🎨 "Should AI create art?"

✅ For

AI makes art creation accessible to millions who can't draw or afford training. It's a tool — like a camera was once considered "cheating" in photography. Great art is about ideas, not just technique.

❌ Against

AI art is trained on human artists' work without consent. It devalues human creativity, takes jobs from working artists, and produces output without genuine emotion or lived experience.

🎯 KEY ISSUE: Consent, credit, and compensation for original artists.

🏛️ Classroom Debate

Split into two teams. Pick ONE topic above. Team A argues FOR, Team B argues AGAINST — even if you personally disagree! Then switch. The best team gives reasons, not just opinions.

The students who understand BOTH sides of AI ethics will be the ones trusted to lead AI projects. Nuance is a superpower that AI cannot learn.

Section 03

My AI Rules 📋

5 personal rules every responsible AI user should follow. Read them, memorise them, live them.

01

🔒 Safety First — Protect Your Privacy

Never share your real name, school, address, or phone number with AI chatbots. Don't upload photos of yourself or others to unknown AI tools. Read privacy policies before using new apps.

02

🕵️ Always Verify — Don't Trust Blindly

AI can be confidently wrong (this is called "hallucination"). Always double-check AI-generated facts using reliable sources like Wikipedia, news sites, or textbooks before sharing or using them.

03

✅ Be Honest — Disclose AI Use

If you used AI to help write something, say so. Don't submit AI work as entirely your own without acknowledgement. Honesty builds trust — and in school and work, trust is everything.

04

💜 Be Kind — Don't Use AI to Harm

Never use AI to create fake content about real people. Never use AI to bully, spread rumours, or create embarrassing images of others. What feels like a joke can destroy someone's life. Choose kindness.

05

🧠 Stay in Control — AI Assists, You Decide

AI is your assistant, not your boss. Always review AI outputs critically. Don't let AI make important decisions for you — about friendships, health, or beliefs. Your judgment matters more than an algorithm.

🌟 My Personal Pledge

Write these 5 rules in your own words in your notebook. Then add a 6th rule of your own — something you personally commit to as an AI user.

A person who uses AI responsibly is more powerful than one who uses it recklessly. Power comes with wisdom. 🧠
