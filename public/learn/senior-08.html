Your Content Calendar 📅

Successful creators don't post randomly — they plan. Here's a simple weekly plan you can actually stick to.

🧠

What to Post (and Why)

The 3 types of content every creator needs:
• Educate — teach something (gets shared)
• Entertain — make people smile/laugh (gets likes)
• Inspire — motivate or tell a story (builds loyal followers)
Mix all 3 for the best results!

AI will give you 3–5 options in seconds. Edit, personalise, and post!
