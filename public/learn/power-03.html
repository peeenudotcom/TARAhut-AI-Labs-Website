⚠️

Common Mistake: Submitting Raw AI Text

Never submit AI output without personalizing it. AI gives you the structure and language. YOU must add:
• Your real name, college, company names
• Specific experiences that are true
• Personal details AI can't know
• A final proofread for accuracy

"AI first draft denda hai. Tuhada kaam hai — personalize karo, check karo, phir bhejo."
