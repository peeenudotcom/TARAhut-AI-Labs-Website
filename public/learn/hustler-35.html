Portfolio

Finalize Your Portfolio

Your portfolio is your proof of work. Every future client will judge you by what they see here. Take 30 minutes to make it perfect.

📁Must Include

Client Work Samples

GMB profiles you set up, social media posts you created, websites you built. Before/after screenshots with metrics.

💬Must Include

Testimonials

At least 2-3 client testimonials. Video is best, screenshots are good. Real names and businesses if they consent.

📊Must Include

Results & Metrics

GMB views increase, social media reach, website traffic. Numbers make you credible. "200 views in first month" beats "good results."

Portfolio Finalization Checklist

  • Organize all work samples by service type (GMB, Social, Web, Ads)
  • Add before/after screenshots for each project
  • Include metrics wherever possible (views, clicks, followers, revenue)
  • Add 2-3 testimonials (video or text)
  • Create a 1-page PDF summary for quick sharing on WhatsApp
  • Save everything in a Google Drive folder with a shareable link
Portfolio finalized — your professional proof of work is ready

Presentation Tips

Show, don't tell: Open the actual GMB profile, show the social media page, demo the website live. Be specific: "200 views in 3 weeks" not "good results." Be honest: Share challenges too — what was hard, what you learned. Be proud: You earned this.

Earning Tracker Review

  • Total up all income earned during the program
  • Note your highest-earning service and biggest client
