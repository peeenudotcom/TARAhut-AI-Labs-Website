CapCut Web vs CapCut Mobile

📱

Use Both — They Serve Different Purposes

CapCut Mobile (Phone App): Best for quick Reels — auto-captions, trending templates, fast export. Film on phone, edit on phone, post in 10 minutes.

CapCut Web (capcut.com): Full desktop editing suite. Better for longer videos, brand color control, more advanced AI tools like text-to-video and smart scene detection. Use this for client work.

The 15-Minute Reel Workflow

1

Film or Generate Raw Footage (3 min)

Film yourself talking about a product/service on your phone (1–2 minutes). OR use a HeyGen AI avatar video. OR use CapCut's text-to-video with a script.

2

Import & Auto-Caption (2 min)

Open CapCut → Upload → Click "Captions" → Select style (bold, modern, minimal). Auto-generates captions. Review quickly for errors (usually very accurate for English and Hindi).

3

Apply Template or Music (3 min)

Browse templates → pick one trending in your niche. Or add background music from CapCut's royalty-free library. Adjust volume so voice is louder than music.

4

Smart Edit & Polish (5 min)

AI Smart Edit removes pauses and filler. Enhance video quality. Add B-roll clips from CapCut's free stock library. Add your logo as a watermark.

5

Export & Post (2 min)

Export at 1080p or 4K. Share directly to Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts from CapCut. Or download and post manually for more control over captions and hashtags.

Step 1: Script with Claude (10 minutes)

Prompt Claude: "Write a 60-second video script for [business name], a [type of business] in Kotkapura. The video should introduce the business, highlight 3 key services, and end with a clear call to action to WhatsApp at [number]. Tone: friendly and professional. Target audience: local families." Copy the script. Edit any local references.

Step 2: Avatar Video with HeyGen (15 minutes)

Paste the Claude script into HeyGen. Choose an avatar that matches the business personality (formal for clinic, warm for restaurant). Select a voice in the right language. Set a professional background. Click Generate — your 60-second presenter video renders in 5–10 minutes.

Step 3: Edit & Polish with CapCut (15 minutes)

Import the HeyGen video into CapCut. Add auto-captions. Add B-roll clips of the business (stock footage or real photos made into a slideshow). Add background music at 20% volume. Add logo watermark. Add an end card with contact details. Apply color grading for cinematic feel.

Step 4: Final Video (Ready to Publish)

Export at 1080p. You now have: a complete 60-second brand video with professional presenter, captions, music, B-roll, logo, and CTA. This video can be used on Instagram Reels, YouTube, WhatsApp Business, and the business website. Total cost: ~₹0-500 in tool fees. Total time: 40-45 minutes.

One Video, Multiple Languages
