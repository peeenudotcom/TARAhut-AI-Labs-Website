The Simplest Mental Model

IF something happens → THEN do something automatically.
"IF koi Google Form bhare → THEN unnu WhatsApp message jaave."
"IF koi email aave → THEN AI summary bane → THEN Google Sheet vich save hove."
That's it. Trigger → Action → Result.
