What Career Suits You? 🎯

Answer 5 quick questions and discover which AI-era career paths match your personality and strengths!

Why This Matters

Most people pick careers based on what their parents did or what sounds cool. But the best career is one that matches how YOUR brain naturally works. Let's find yours.

QUESTION 1 of 5

When you get a free afternoon, what do you naturally do?
🎨 Draw, make videos, write stories, or create something
📊 Research things, watch documentaries, or solve puzzles
💬 Hang out with friends, organise plans, or lead activities
🔧 Build, code, fix things, or experiment with tech

QUESTION 2 of 5

Which school subject do you actually enjoy (even a little)?
✏️ Art, English, or Drama
📐 Maths, Science, or Commerce
🌍 Social Studies, History, or Economics

QUESTION 3 of 5

You're doing a group project. What role do you naturally take?
🎬 Making it look good — designs, slides, presentation

🗣️ Presenting it, getting the team together, leading

QUESTION 4 of 5

What kind of work sounds most exciting to you?
🎯 Making content, ads, or media that people see
📈 Finding patterns in data to help companies decide
🤝 Working with people to solve problems or sell ideas

QUESTION 5 of 5

If you could be famous for one thing, what would it be?
📱 My YouTube/Instagram content that millions follow
🏆 The person who spotted a trend before anyone else

Section 03

Build Your Student Portfolio 💼

A portfolio is proof that you can do what you say you can. Even as a student, you can build one that impresses anyone.

🌟

What Is a Portfolio?

It's your highlight reel — a collection of things you've made, learned, or achieved. It's like a resume, but for the real world. Companies and colleges both look at portfolios now.

What to Include in Your Student Portfolio

01

About Me (Self-Introduction)

2–3 sentences: who you are, what you're passionate about, and what you're learning. Keep it confident and clear.

02

Your Skills

List tools you know (Canva, ChatGPT, Google Slides), subjects you're good at, languages you speak. Even basic skills count!

03

Projects & Work Samples

Any project from school, this course, or personal time. A blog post, an infographic, a presentation, a short video — all count.

04

Achievements & Certificates

School awards, sports achievements, online course certificates (like TARAhut AI Explorer!), anything that shows you've done something.

05

Goals & What You're Learning Next

Shows that you're ambitious and always growing. "Currently learning data analysis" sounds more impressive than a blank space.

📝 Example Self-Introduction

I'm passionate about [your interest — e.g., data, content creation, tech] and I'm currently completing the TARAhut AI Explorer course to build real AI skills. My goal is to become a [your dream career] and I'm building my portfolio one project at a time."
