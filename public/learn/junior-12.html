Presentation Tips — One Last Time!
Quick reminders before you take the stage! You've got this! 💪
😊

Smile First!

Walk up, pause, look at everyone, and give your biggest smile. It instantly relaxes you AND your audience!

🌬️

Breathe!

If nerves hit, take one deep breath before you start. Breathe in for 4 counts, out for 4. You'll feel calmer instantly!

🏆

Be Proud!

You made something real with AI. Not everyone can say that! Walk up there knowing you deserve to be there! ⭐

🐢

Slow Down!

When nervous, we speak too fast. Slow down, say every word clearly, and pause after important points!

👀

Look Up!

Don't just read from the screen. Look at your audience! Move your gaze around — make everyone feel included!

🎉

Enjoy It!

This is YOUR moment. Something you worked really hard for. Whatever happens, be proud and ENJOY it!

💡 The secret truth about presenting: The audience WANTS you to do well! They're on your side. They're excited to see what you made! Remember that when you walk up. 🌟
