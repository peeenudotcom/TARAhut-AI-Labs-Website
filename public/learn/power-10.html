Build Your Custom GPT

Step-by-step: create an AI assistant tailored to YOUR needs. This is where your CRISP prompting skills truly shine.

🤖 Demo: "Punjab Business Advisor" GPT

Follow these steps in ChatGPT → Explore GPTs → Create:

📋 Click to copy GPT Instructions: You are a friendly business advisor who understands Punjab's economy. You speak in simple Hinglish. You give practical, low-cost advice suitable for small businesses in tier-2/3 Punjab cities. Always suggest free or very affordable tools. Give examples from retail, agriculture, education, and healthcare sectors.

Paste this as the GPT's Instructions. Add a name, description, and 4 conversation starters. Test it live!

Custom GPT Ideas for Students

STUDENT🎓

IELTS Writing Coach

A GPT that grades IELTS essays, gives band scores, and suggests improvements. "You are an IELTS examiner. Grade my writing and suggest improvements for Band 7+."

BUSINESS🛒

Customer Query Handler

Handle common customer questions for any business. "You handle customer inquiries for [business name]. Answer about products, prices, timings, and delivery."

FARMER🌾

Punjab Farming Advisor

Crop advice, mandi prices, government schemes. "You advise Punjab farmers on crops, weather, MSP, and government schemes. Speak in simple Punjabi-Hindi mix."

JOB SEEKER💼

Interview Prep Coach

Practice interviews for any role. "You are an HR manager. Interview me for [role] at a [industry] company. Ask questions one by one, rate my answers."

💡 Demo: AI-Written GPT Description

Let AI write the perfect description for your Custom GPT:

📋 Click to copy: Write a compelling description for a Custom GPT called [YOUR GPT NAME]. It helps [TARGET USER] with [WHAT IT DOES]. Include what makes it unique and 4 conversation starter questions.

Use AI to write about your AI. Meta, but effective.

Build Your GPT NOW

Don't just read — do it right now:

  • Open ChatGPT → Explore GPTs → Create
  • Pick a name (IELTS Coach, Business Advisor, your own idea)
  • Paste your instructions using CRISP framework
  • Add 4 conversation starters
  • Test it with 3 different questions. Refine the instructions.
