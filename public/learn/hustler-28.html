Section 02 — Pitch Scripts

The Approach Script Toolkit

Three versions of your opening pitch. Practice each one 3 times out loud before you leave this room. The words must feel natural, not robotic.

Opening Pitch: Standard (30 Seconds)

Click to copy: "Namaste ji, mera naam [Your Name] hai, main TARAhut AI Labs se digital marketing consultant hoon. Kya main aapko 2 minute mein bataa sakta/sakti hoon ki hum aapke business ki online presence kaise badha sakte hain?"
This is your default opener. It is polite, professional, and asks for permission. The '2 minute' time limit reduces resistance. Practice this 3 times until it flows naturally.

Opening Pitch: Compliment-First (More Warm)

Click to copy: "Namaste ji! Aapki shop bahut achhi hai. Main [Your Name], TARAhut AI Labs se digital marketing mein kaam karta/karti hoon. Maine dekha aapka Google pe profile nahi hai -- main aapko dikha sakta/sakti hoon ki 10 minute mein kaise bana sakte hain. Free mein."
Compliment-first works great with shop owners who take pride in their business. The 'free' hook lowers the barrier. You are offering something before asking for anything.

Opening Pitch: Problem-Led (Direct)

Click to copy: "Namaste ji, kya aapke business ka Instagram ya Google pe profile hai? Nahi? Main yeh kaam karta/karti hoon -- chhote businesses ko online le jaata/jaati hoon. Mera naam [Your Name] hai, TARAhut AI Labs se hoon. Ek sample dikhaata/dikhaati hoon 30 second mein."
Problem-led works with businesses that clearly have zero online presence. You identify the gap immediately and position yourself as the solution. Show the portfolio sample within 30 seconds.

Top 5 Objections You Will Hear Today

OBJECTION 1🙅

"Humein zaroorat nahi hai"

Response: "Ji bilkul, bahut se business owners yeh bolte hain. Lekin aapke competitor [next door/nearby] ne social media shuru kiya hai. Kya main aapko ek free sample bana ke bhej sakta hoon? Dekhne mein kya jaata hai."

OBJECTION 2💸

"Budget nahi hai"

Response: "Main samajh sakta hoon. Isiliye hamara starter plan sirf ₹3,000/month hai -- ek din ka ₹100. Aur pehle hum aapko free sample dikhayenge. Pasand aaye toh aage badhenge."

OBJECTION 3🤔

"Sochke batata hoon"

Response: "Bilkul, sochiye. Main apna card chhod jaata hoon. Kya main aapko kal ek free sample post bana ke WhatsApp pe bhej sakta hoon? Dekhne mein kya jaata hai."

OBJECTION 4🏢

"Aapki company kya hai?"

Response: "TARAhut AI Labs -- hum AI se digital marketing services dete hain chhote businesses ko. Yeh hamara card hai aur yeh hain hamare past projects." (Show portfolio on phone)

OBJECTION 5📂

"Pehle portfolio dikhao"

Response: "Zaroor! Yeh dekhiye." (Show phone immediately with 3-4 best designs) "Yeh ek salon ke liye banaya, yeh ek restaurant ke liye. Aapke business ke liye bhi aisa ban sakta hai."

💡

Pro Tip: The 2-Minute Rule

Never talk for more than 2 minutes. After your pitch, stop and ask a question. 'Aapke business ka sabse bada challenge kya hai online mein?' Let them talk. People who talk feel heard, and people who feel heard are more likely to say yes.

Role Play Practice (Do This Now)

Pair up. One person is the business owner, one is the consultant. Round 1: Owner says 'Humein zaroorat nahi hai' -- Consultant responds. Round 2: Owner says 'Budget nahi hai' -- Consultant responds. Round 3: Owner says 'Sochke batata hoon' -- Consultant responds. Switch roles. Repeat. You must be able to handle these 3 objections smoothly before stepping out.

Zones, Pairs & Safety Rules

Every pair gets a zone on the market map. Targets, rules, and the trainer accompaniment plan. Study this before heading out.

Field Work Targets

TARGET🏪

5+ Businesses Per Pair

Minimum 5 businesses approached. If you finish 5 early and still have time, go for 7. More conversations = more chances = more confidence.

COLLECT📱

Contact Info

For every business visited: owner name, phone number (if shared), WhatsApp status (interested / maybe / not interested). This becomes your follow-up list.

DELIVER📄

Service Menu

Hand over your printed service menu to every business you visit. Even if they say no. They might look at it later. Plant the seed.

ASSESS📝

Quick Notes

After each visit, write quick notes: what did they say? Any specific needs mentioned? What objection? This information is gold for follow-ups.

🛡️

Safety Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. Stay in pairs at all times. 2. Share live location on WhatsApp group. 3. Trainer's phone number on speed dial. 4. If you feel unsafe or uncomfortable at any business, leave immediately. 5. Return to classroom by the exact return time. 6. Check in via WhatsApp every 20 minutes with a quick update.

At Each Business: The 5-Step Process

  • Step 1: Walk in confidently. Ask for the owner. Smile.
  • Step 2: Deliver your 30-second opening pitch.
  • Step 3: Show 1 portfolio sample on your phone (30 seconds max).
  • Step 4: Hand over the service menu. Let them look at it.
  • Step 5: Ask: "Kya main aapko ek free sample post bana ke bhej sakta hoon?" Collect contact if yes or maybe.

Common Day 1 Mistakes to Avoid

1. Talking too much -- deliver your 2-minute pitch, then let THEM talk. 2. Getting discouraged after first rejection -- rejection is normal, even the best salespeople get rejected 7 out of 10 times. 3. Skipping businesses because they look "too big" or "too small" -- every business is a prospect. 4. Not collecting contact info -- even from businesses that say no, ask "Kya main aapko ek free sample bhej sakta hoon? Number mil jayega?"

Every "no" brings you closer to a "yes." The math of sales is simple: more approaches = more income.
