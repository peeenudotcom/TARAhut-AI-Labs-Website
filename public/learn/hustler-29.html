Top 3 Mistakes from Day 28 (Fix These Today)

MISTAKE 1👀

Not Making Eye Contact

Look the business owner in the eye when you speak. Confidence is communicated through body language more than words. Stand straight. Smile. Eye contact = trust.

MISTAKE 2🏃

Rushing Through the Pitch

Slow down. Breathe between sentences. A rushed pitch sounds desperate. A calm pitch sounds professional. Pause after your opening line. Let them process.

MISTAKE 3📱

Not Asking for the Number

Even if they say "no" to the service, ask: "Kya main aapko ek free sample bhej sakta hoon? WhatsApp number mil jayega?" Getting the number keeps the door open.

Section 02 — Refined Pitch

Improved Pitch Based on Day 28

Yesterday taught us what works and what does not. Today's pitch is sharper, more natural, and addresses the objections we actually heard.

Refined Opening (Co-Created with Class)

Click to copy: "Namaste ji! Mera naam [Your Name] hai. Main chhote businesses ki online marketing karta/karti hoon -- Instagram, Google, WhatsApp sab. Aapka 2 minute le sakta/sakti hoon? Main dikhata/dikhati hoon ki aapke jaise businesses kya kar rahe hain online."
Key improvements from Day 28: (1) Removed 'TARAhut AI Labs' from opening -- say it only if they ask, (2) Added 'aapke jaise businesses' -- creates curiosity about competition, (3) More natural language, less scripted feel.

The Show-Don't-Tell Approach

Click to copy: "Ji dekhiye, yeh maine ek [similar business type] ke liye banaya hai. [Show phone with portfolio] Unka Instagram pehle kuch nahi tha, ab 500+ followers hain 2 mahine mein. Aapke liye bhi aisa ho sakta hai. Ek sample bana ke bhejoon?"
Show the portfolio BEFORE explaining your services. A picture is worth a thousand words. Business owners respond to visual proof, not verbal promises. Have your best 3 designs ready to show instantly.

New Objection Responses (From Day 28 Experience)

NEW🏢

"Aapka experience kya hai?"

Response: "Main AI tools se kaam karta hoon jo Fortune 500 companies use karti hain. Yeh dekhiye mere portfolio mein -- [show phone]. Main aapko pehle free sample bana ke dikhata hoon. Usse judge kariye."

NEW👤

"Mera beta/bhatija kar leta hai"

Response: "Ji bilkul, lekin kya woh regularly kar pata hai? Zyaadatar businesses mein yeh problem hoti hai ki shuru karte hain phir band ho jaata hai. Hamara kaam consistent hai -- har hafte content milta hai."

NEW📅

"Abhi nahi, baad mein aana"

Response: "Ji zaroor. Main apna card chhod jaata hoon. Kab aana chahiye? [Get specific day/time]. Main tab aaunga aur ek free sample bhi le ke aaunga aapke business ka."

NEW🤷

"Online se kya hoga, hamara kaam offline chalta hai"

Response: "Ji sahi kaha. Lekin Google pe search kariye '[their business type] near me' -- jo businesses dikhte hain unhe zyada customers milte hain. Main aapko Google pe dikha sakta hoon. Free hai."

🔑

The Secret Weapon: Free Sample Offer

The most powerful words you can say today: 'Main aapke liye ek free sample post bana ke WhatsApp pe bhej dunga. Dekhne mein kya jaata hai.' This does three things: (1) Gets you the WhatsApp number, (2) Shows your skills without risk, (3) Creates a reason to follow up. Use it at every single business today.

Quick Practice Round (2 Minutes)

Stand up. Pair with someone. Practice the refined pitch ONCE each. Focus on:

  • Eye contact throughout
  • Slow, confident delivery
  • Show phone with portfolio DURING the pitch
  • End with the free sample offer

Section 03 — Solo Rules

Solo Sprint Rules & Targets

You go alone to your assigned zone. Target: 5+ businesses. Check in every 20 minutes. Take a photo of each storefront visited.

⚠️

Solo Safety Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. Share live location on WhatsApp group before leaving. 2. Check in every 20 minutes: "[Name] -- [count] done, [interested count] interested." 3. Trainer's phone on speed dial. 4. If uncomfortable, return to class immediately. No questions asked. 5. If a prospect is VERY interested and you need backup, message trainer: "HOT LEAD at [location]" -- trainer will come.

The Solo Approach: 7-Step Process

  • Step 1: Walk in confidently. Ask for the owner. Smile and make eye contact.
  • Step 2: Deliver the refined 2-minute pitch (slower than yesterday).
  • Step 3: Show portfolio sample on phone -- your BEST work, right away.
  • Step 4: Hand over service menu. Let them look.
  • Step 5: Ask: "Kya main aapko ek free sample post bana ke bhej sakta hoon?"
  • Step 6: Collect contact number (WhatsApp).
  • Step 7: Thank them. Take a quick storefront photo (for your records). Move to next.
TARGET🏪

5+ Businesses Each

Minimum 5. If you hit 5 early, keep going. 7 makes you a champion. 10 makes you a legend. Volume builds confidence AND creates pipeline.

TRACK📸

Storefront Photos

Take a quick photo of each business storefront. This serves as your visual record and also helps you remember which shop was which during debrief.

SEND📱

WhatsApp Check-Ins

Every 20 minutes: "[Name] -- 3 done, 1 interested. Going strong!" Trainer responds to EVERY check-in with encouragement.

For Extremely Nervous Students

Pair mein jaoge toh hamesha doosre pe rely karoge. Akele jaoge toh confidence build hoga.
