How to Check Facts Like a Pro!
3 simple steps to know if something is true or false
1

📖 READ It Carefully

Read the whole thing — not just the headline! Sometimes the headline sounds scary but the full story is totally normal. Don't share something until you've actually read it!

2

🔎 CHECK with Google or Perplexity

Open Google or Perplexity AI and search for the same fact. Does a trusted website (like BBC, National Geographic, or your school website) say the same thing? If yes — it's probably real!

3

🙋 VERIFY with a Grown-Up

Still not sure? Ask a parent or teacher! They can help you figure out if something is true. Two brains are better than one! 🧠🧠

💡 Top Tip: If something sounds TOO amazing or TOO scary — it's probably fake! Real facts are usually interesting but not super shocking.

🟢 Trust These Sites

BBC, National Geographic, NASA, Wikipedia (for basic facts), your school's website, government websites (.gov)

🔴 Be Careful With

WhatsApp forwards, unknown blogs, websites with lots of pop-ups, anything that says "SHARE THIS NOW!" in big letters
