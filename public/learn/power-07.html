AI for Retail & Agriculture

From kiryana stores and clothing shops to wheat fields and dairy farms — AI solutions that work in Punjab's real economy.

Sector 1: Retail & Wholesale

🛒

Kiryana Store / Clothing Shop / Arhti

Every retail business in Punjab faces the same problems: customer communication, marketing, and record keeping. AI solves all three in minutes, not months.

🧪 Demo: WhatsApp Reply Templates for Retail

Use AI to create 10 professional WhatsApp reply templates for a kiryana store in 2 minutes:

📋 Click to copy: Write 10 WhatsApp quick reply templates for a kiryana store in Punjab. Include replies for: rate inquiry, stock availability, delivery questions, payment modes, bulk order inquiry, and festival offers. Keep each reply under 50 words. Mix Hindi and Punjabi naturally.

"Rate ki hai?", "Stock hai?", "Delivery hundi hai?" — all answered professionally in seconds.

🧪 Demo: Festival Sale Marketing

Use Canva AI to create a Diwali sale poster in 60 seconds, then generate broadcast messages:

📋 Click to copy: Generate 5 WhatsApp broadcast messages for a Diwali sale announcement at a clothing store in Bathinda, Punjab. Include discounts, store timings, and festive greetings. Use warm Punjabi-Hindi mix. Add relevant emojis. Each message under 100 words.

5 professional marketing messages ready to broadcast — free, instant.

🧪 Demo: Inventory Tracker with AI

Ask AI to create a complete inventory system in Google Sheets:

📋 Click to copy: Create a simple inventory tracker for a kiryana store in Google Sheets. Columns: item name, category, quantity in stock, buying price, selling price, reorder level, supplier name. Add formulas for profit margin and a low-stock alert column that shows RED when quantity is below reorder level.

A complete digital inventory system — no coding needed, just copy-paste formulas.

Ask AI for personalized farming advice based on your specific conditions:

📋 Click to copy: I am a wheat farmer in Faridkot, Punjab. My soil is sandy loam. What should I plant after wheat harvest? Consider water availability and market prices. Give me top 5 options with expected yield and market price per quintal.

"Bookmark this prompt. Use it daily." — Real money saved by choosing the right mandi.
