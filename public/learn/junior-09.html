Spot the Fake!
AI can create fake photos, videos, and text — here's how to stay sharp!

🛡️ Your AI Safety Rules

📸

Fake Photos Exist!

AI can make photos of things that NEVER happened. Always ask: "Is this from a real news site?"

🎙️

Fake Voices Too!

AI can copy someone's voice. If you get a scary phone call from an "unknown relative" — tell an adult!

📰

Fake News is Real!

AI can write fake news articles that look totally real. Always check if a trusted website reports the same story!

🤝

Fake "Friends" Online!

Some online accounts are AI bots pretending to be people. Never share personal details with people you only know online!

🔒

Private = Private!

Your full name, school, address, and phone number are PRIVATE. Keep them that way — online and offline!

👩‍👧

Tell a Grown-Up!

If anything online makes you uncomfortable or scared — ALWAYS tell a parent or teacher. Never keep it a secret!

🟢 3 Signs Something is REAL

• It's from a website you know and trust
• Other news sources report the same thing
• The photos look normal (not perfect or weird)

🔴 3 Signs Something is FAKE

• It sounds too shocking or too good
• Only ONE website or message says it
• The photo looks strange (extra fingers, blurry edges)

💡 Fun Fact: AI-generated faces often have blurry teeth or extra fingers! Look carefully at photos — nature's details are hard for AI to get perfectly right! 🖐️
