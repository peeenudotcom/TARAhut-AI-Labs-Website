Section 04

Research Chains & Workshop

Chain prompts so output of Step 1 feeds Step 2, which feeds Step 3. This produces analysis no single prompt can match.

💡

What is a Research Chain?

Break research into 3 stages in the SAME conversation:
Step 1 — Research: Gather facts, statistics, trends, key players
Step 2 — Analyse: Identify patterns, gaps, implications from Step 1
Step 3 — Recommend: Provide actionable recommendations grounded in analysis

Each step builds on the previous. Step 3 is dramatically better because it is informed by Steps 1 and 2.

🧪 Demo 5: 3-Step Research Chain

Send these three prompts in sequence in the SAME conversation. Wait for each response before sending the next:

📋 Click to copy: STEP 1 — Research EV landscape in Punjab (send first)
📋 Click to copy: STEP 2 — Analyse patterns and gaps (send after Step 1)
📋 Click to copy: STEP 3 — Recommendations for Kotkapura entrepreneur (send after Step 2)

By Step 3, Claude has built a comprehensive mental model. Recommendations are grounded in research, not generic advice.

⚠

Critical: Same Conversation Only

Research chains ONLY work in the same conversation. New chat for Step 2 = Claude loses all Step 1 context. Same conversation = compounding intelligence. New conversation = starting from zero.
