Section 02

Portfolio Polish

पोर्टफोलियो पॉलिश

ਪੋਰਟਫੋਲੀਓ ਪਾਲਿਸ਼

Not all portfolio pieces are equal. Pick your BEST 5 and polish them to showroom quality. These 5 will be your client-facing samples.

⭐

The "Best 5" Strategy

Clients don't want to see 50 mediocre pieces. They want to see 5 INCREDIBLE pieces that prove you're the right choice. Pick your best work from each category and make it flawless. Better to have 5 perfect pieces than 20 okay ones.

🧪 Prompt: Polish Portfolio Copy with ChatGPT

📋 Copy: Generate professional portfolio descriptions with ChatGPT

Action: Polish Your Best 5

  • Piece 1 (Writing): Your best content package. Fix typos, improve headlines, ensure brand consistency.
  • Piece 2 (Design): Your best Instagram post set. Align elements, check color consistency, verify text readability.
  • Piece 3 (Brand Kit): Your most complete brand kit. Add a 1-page "Brand Guidelines" PDF.
  • Piece 4 (Marketing): Your best flyer or menu. Check print-readiness, ensure high resolution.
  • Piece 5 (Package): Your complete marketing package. This is your hero piece — make it perfect.

15 minutes per piece. 75 minutes total. These 5 pieces will land your first clients.

❌ Unpolished Portfolio

  • Typos in content samples
  • Inconsistent colors across designs
  • Low-resolution exports
  • No project descriptions
  • Random file names ("design_final_v3.png")
  • Missing business context

✅ Polished Portfolio

  • Zero typos, proofread twice
  • Consistent brand colors in every piece
  • High-resolution exports (300 DPI for print, 72 DPI for web)
  • Professional project descriptions
  • Clean file names ("RajuSweets-InstagramPostSet.png")
  • Client context and results included
💡

Portfolio Presentation Formats

Google Drive folder: Organized sub-folders, shareable link (easiest, most common).
Canva Portfolio: Design a visual portfolio as a Canva presentation (most impressive).
Behance: Upload to behance.net for global visibility (best for design work).
WhatsApp PDF: Convert best 5 to single PDF for sharing on WhatsApp (most practical for local clients).
Do ALL FOUR. Different clients, different formats.

"Portfolio polish karna = apne kaam ki izzat karna. Jo apne kaam ko polish karta hai, client usse serious leta hai. Professionalism dikhao."

10 Exam Challenges

10 परीक्षा चैलेंज

10 ਪ੍ਰੀਖਿਆ ਚੈਲੰਜ

Complete each challenge within its time limit. Save all outputs to "Phase 1 Exam" folder.

⏱ 8 MINUTES

Challenge 1: Social Media Content Pack

A new gym called "Iron Fitness, Phagwara" needs social media content. Create:

  • 5 Instagram captions (motivational, offer, tip, testimonial, event)
  • Each caption with relevant hashtags (10-15 per post)
  • CTA in each post (DM, call, visit)
  • At least 1 caption in Hinglish

Deliverable: 5 captions saved in a document. Quality: ready to post.

⏱ 7 MINUTES

Challenge 2: Trilingual WhatsApp Messages

"Raju Sweets, Jalandhar" needs Baisakhi promotion messages. Create:

  • 1 message in English (professional, for corporate orders)
  • 1 message in Hindi Roman/Hinglish (casual, for WhatsApp broadcast)
  • 1 message in Punjabi Roman (warm, for regular customers)
  • Each under 80 words with emojis and CTA

Deliverable: 3 messages. Must feel natural in each language, not translated.

⏱ 10 MINUTES

Challenge 3: Google My Business + Email

"Dr. Sharma Dental Care, Bathinda" needs digital presence content. Create:

  • Google My Business description (750 characters, SEO keywords)
  • Welcome email for new patients (warm, informative, includes services)
  • Follow-up email after first visit (thank you + review request)

Deliverable: 1 GMB description + 2 emails. Professional medical tone.

⏱ 8 MINUTES

Challenge 4: Brand Kit Design

Create a complete Brand Kit for "Kaur Boutique, Amritsar" (women's clothing).

  • 5 brand colors with HEX codes (use ChatGPT)
  • 2 font suggestions with reasoning
  • Set up Brand Kit in Canva (colors + fonts)
  • Create a 1-page Brand Guidelines visual

Deliverable: Brand Kit set up in Canva + Brand Guidelines PNG.

⏱ 10 MINUTES

Challenge 5: Instagram Post Set

Using the Kaur Boutique brand kit, design 3 Instagram posts:

  • Post 1: New Arrival announcement (product showcase)
  • Post 2: Customer testimonial with star rating
  • Post 3: Festival sale (Baisakhi special)
  • All posts: 1080x1080, brand colors, CTA, readable text

Deliverable: 3 Instagram post PNGs. Consistent brand identity across all.

⏱ 8 MINUTES

Challenge 6: Story Template Pack

Create 3 Instagram Story templates for Kaur Boutique:

  • Story 1: "This or That" poll (two outfit styles)
  • Story 2: Behind-the-scenes (new collection sneak peek)
  • Story 3: Offer countdown (48-hour flash sale)
  • All: 1080x1920, brand colors, space for stickers

Deliverable: 3 Story template PNGs. Reusable by client daily.

⏱ 10 MINUTES

Challenge 7: Marketing Flyer

Design a Grand Opening flyer for a new restaurant: "Tandoor Express, Ludhiana".

  • A4 size, print-ready (PDF Print export)
  • Includes: business name, opening date, address, inaugural offer
  • Phone number (large), Google Maps QR code
  • Warm, appetizing color scheme
  • Both print PDF and digital PNG versions

Deliverable: 1 flyer PDF + 1 flyer PNG. Ready to send to printer.

⏱ 10 MINUTES

Challenge 8: WhatsApp Catalog

Create 5 WhatsApp catalog images for Tandoor Express restaurant:

  • 5 menu items with food descriptions and prices
  • Consistent template across all 5
  • 1024x1024 px, clean backgrounds, price badges
  • Veg/Non-veg indicators, spice levels
  • Business branding at bottom

Deliverable: 5 catalog PNGs. Consistent, appetizing, ready for WhatsApp Business.

⏱ 10 MINUTES

Challenge 9: Business Card + Flyer Combo

Create a business card AND service flyer for yourself as a freelancer:

  • Business card: front + back, your name, "Digital Marketing & Design"
  • Service flyer: A5, list your services, pricing tiers, contact, QR code
  • Both must share the same brand identity (your personal brand)

Deliverable: 1 business card (front+back) + 1 service flyer. Your actual marketing materials.

⏱ 9 MINUTES

Challenge 10: Speed Sprint — Complete Mini-Package

A coaching center "Bright Academy, Mohali" needs an urgent mini-package. Create ALL of these in 9 minutes:

  • 1 Instagram post (admissions open)
  • 1 Instagram story template (results announcement)
  • 1 WhatsApp broadcast message (Hinglish)
  • Must use consistent branding across all 3

Deliverable: 3 pieces in 9 minutes. This tests your commercial speed. GO!
