Section 02

Showcase Guide 🎤

Everything you need to know for the next few hours. This is your moment — own it!

📋 Showcase Format

1

Welcome & Introduction (5 min)

Your teacher/facilitator opens the Showcase and introduces the format

2

Student Presentations (3–5 min each)

Each student presents their Capstone Project. Listen and support your classmates!

3

Q&A After Each Presentation (2 min)

Audience and judges can ask questions. Be ready — and be brave!

4

Peer Recognition (5 min)

Classmates nominate each other for achievements — most creative, most useful, most improved

5

🎓 Graduation Ceremony

Certificate presentation, photos, celebrate TOGETHER!

🎤

When It's YOUR Turn

Walk to the front confidently. Take one deep breath. Start with your hook — the opening that grabs attention. Remember: speak slowly, make eye contact, smile. The audience wants you to succeed. They're rooting for you.

👂

When Others Are Presenting

Be the audience you wish you had. Put your phone away. Listen genuinely. Give encouragement (a nod, a smile). Think of ONE question to ask. Being a good audience member is as important as being a good presenter.

😅

If You Feel Nervous

Take three slow, deep breaths before you start. Remember: it's OK to be nervous — everyone is. Your classmates are not judging you — they know exactly how you feel because they feel it too. If you lose your place, pause, take a breath, and continue. Nobody will mind the pause.
