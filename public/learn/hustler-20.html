WhatsApp Business Power Features

WhatsApp is the #1 communication tool in India. Every business customer is on WhatsApp. Yet most businesses use personal WhatsApp instead of Business. That is your opportunity.

FEATURE 1📱

Business Profile

Professional photo, business description, address, hours, email, website link. Like a mini website on WhatsApp. First impression that builds trust instantly.

FEATURE 2🛍️

Product Catalog

Products/services with images, descriptions, and prices. Customers browse like a shop. No more sending individual photos. Looks professional and organized.

FEATURE 3⚡

Quick Replies

10 pre-written responses for common questions: greeting, pricing, hours, location, booking, thank you, review request. Save hours of typing the same messages.

FEATURE 4🏷️

Contact Labels

Organize contacts: New Lead, Active Client, Past Client, Hot Lead, Follow-Up. Your CRM inside WhatsApp. Know exactly who each contact is.

FEATURE 5📢

Broadcast Lists

Send messages to 256 contacts at once. Rules: recipients must have your number saved. Best practice: 1-2 broadcasts per week maximum. Offer value, not spam.

FEATURE 6📊

WhatsApp Status

Daily updates visible for 24 hours. Behind-the-scenes, offers, new arrivals, testimonials. Like Instagram Stories but your entire customer base sees it.

Quick Replies + Contact Labels

10 essential quick replies save hours of repetitive typing. Labels organize your contacts into actionable groups.

The 10 Essential Quick Replies

QR 1-2👋

Greeting + Thank You

"Namaste! [Business Name] mein aapka swagat hai. Kaise help kar sakte hain?" and "Thank you for choosing us! Aapka order tayaar hai."

QR 3-4💰

Price Inquiry + Hours

"Hamare rates ke liye catalog dekhein" (send catalog) and "Hum [hours] open hain. Address: [location]."

QR 5-6📍

Location + Booking

Google Maps link with directions and "Appointment book karne ke liye yeh form bharein: [link]."

QR 7-8⭐

Review Request + Follow-Up

"Aapko hamari service kaisi lagi? Google pe review dein: [link]" and "Aapka order/visit kaisa raha? Koi feedback?"

QR 9-10🎉

Festival + Closing

Festival greeting template and "Thank you for contacting us. Jab zaroorat ho, message karein. Hum hamesha available hain."

Section 04

Broadcast Campaigns + Status Marketing

Reach hundreds of customers instantly with broadcasts. Stay top-of-mind with daily Status updates.

⚠️

Broadcast Rules

Maximum 256 contacts per list. Recipients MUST have your number saved in their contacts — otherwise the message will not be delivered. Best practice: 1-2 broadcasts per week. Always provide value. Never spam.

Demo 5: Diwali Broadcast Campaign

Click to copy: "Create 4 WhatsApp broadcast message templates for a sweet shop during Diwali: (1) Diwali collection launch announcement with special prices, (2) Early bird discount offer (order before date), (3) Gift box promotion for corporate orders, (4) Thank you message after Diwali with post-festival offer. Each message under 300 characters. Include emojis. Hindi-English mix. Include a call-to-action in each."

SAT-SUN📋
