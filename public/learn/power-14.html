Section 02

Build Your Own AI Agents

AI agents don't just answer — they DO. Research agents, content agents, customer service agents. Build one today using tools you already know.

💬

Regular AI (Chat)

You ask a question, AI answers. You copy-paste. You do the next step manually. One task at a time.

✕ Manual Steps
🤖

AI Agent (Autonomous)

You give a goal. AI searches, summarizes, writes, emails, schedules, posts — all automatically. Chain of tasks, zero manual work.

✓ Fully Automated

Three Agent Blueprints

AGENT 1🔬

Research Agent

Prompt chain: "Search latest news about [topic]" → "Summarize top 5 results" → "Write a 200-word brief" → "Email it to me." Built with Custom GPT + Zapier.

AGENT 2📝

Content Agent

Full pipeline: Give a topic → AI writes blog → AI creates social posts → AI designs image → Scheduled to publish. Content creation on autopilot.

AGENT 3🛎️

Customer Service Agent

Combined system: Custom GPT + Botpress + Zapier. Customer asks → Bot answers → If stuck → Notifies human → Logs everything automatically.

Research Agent Prompt Chain

Build a research agent step by step. Copy each prompt in sequence:

📋 Step 1: Search for the latest developments in AI automation tools for small businesses in India. Focus on news from the last 30 days. List the top 5 most important developments with source links.
📋 Step 2: Based on the research above, write a 200-word executive brief suitable for a Punjab business owner. Use simple language. Include 3 action items they can implement this week.
📋 Step 3: Convert that brief into a professional email with subject line. Tone: helpful consultant. Include a call to action to schedule a 15-minute consultation. Sign off as [Your Name], AI Consultant.

"Ih teen steps — research, summary, email — pehle 2 ghante launde si. Agent naal 5 minute."

Content Agent Workflow Builder

Design a content creation pipeline that runs itself:

📋 I want to build an AI content agent. My niche is [e.g., AI tips for Punjab businesses]. Create a workflow that takes one topic and produces: (1) a 500-word blog post, (2) three Instagram captions with hashtags, (3) one LinkedIn post, and (4) an image description for Canva. Show me the complete prompt chain I should use.

One topic in, four pieces of content out. "Content factory ban gayi tuhadi."

Build Your First Agent NOW

Choose one agent blueprint above based on your capstone project. Build the prompt chain. Test it end to end. If you chose a business solution capstone, build the Research Agent. If you chose content brand, build the Content Agent.

⚠️

Keep It Simple

AI agents sound complex but they're just prompt chains + automation. You already know both. Don't overcomplicate — the best agents solve ONE problem really well. "Simple agent jo kaam kare > complex agent jo crash kare."

You just designed an AI agent. Most tech professionals haven't done this yet. You're ahead of them.

✓ Universal Access

Voice AI Demo: Talk to ChatGPT

Try having a voice conversation with AI in Punjabi/Hindi:

📋 Open ChatGPT app on your phone. Tap the headphone icon. Say in Punjabi: "Meri ik choti si dukaan hai Kotkapura vich, kapde vechi aan. Dassi ki AI naal main apna business kivein vadha sakdaan?" Listen to AI's response in your language.

"Sochdo — tuhade daddy ji vi phone te AI naal Punjabi vich gall kar sakde ne. Ih accessibility hai."

Voice-to-Action Workflow

Convert voice notes into professional content:

📋 I'm going to paste a voice transcription of my business meeting notes (in Hinglish). Please: 1) Clean up the language, 2) Extract key action items, 3) Create a professional summary email, 4) List follow-up tasks with deadlines. Here are my notes: [paste transcription]

Record → Transcribe → AI processes → Professional output. Voice-first workflow.
