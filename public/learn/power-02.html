The CRISP Framework

5 letters. 5 elements. The backbone of every great AI interaction. "Ih framework puri course da foundation hai."

Not every prompt needs all 5. But knowing all 5 means you always know how to get better results.
C — CONTEXT🌍

Tell AI Who You Are

Example: "Main ik kiryana store owner haan Moga, Punjab vich. Meri shop te 200 customers aunde ne daily."

Why: Without context, AI gives generic answers. With context, it personalizes. "Tussi AI nu apne baare ki dasseya?"

R — ROLE🎭

Assign AI an Expertise

Example: "Tu ik experienced marketing consultant hai jo small businesses di help karda hai."

Why: Role-assignment dramatically changes quality. A "teacher" explains differently than an "expert." Show 3 roles, see 3 different answers.

I — INSTRUCTIONS📋

Give Clear, Specific Instructions

Example: "Meri shop lai 10 Diwali sale slogans likh. Punjabi vich. Witty tone. Under 10 words each."

Why: Vague = vague answers. Specific = specific answers. "Detail deo, detail miluga."

S — SAMPLES📝

Show Examples of Good Output

Example: "Is tarah di style vich likh: 'Diwali di lakh lakh vadhaaiyaan — Moga di #1 shop ton.' Similar feel de."

Why: Examples anchor AI's style and format. Without samples, AI guesses your preference.

P — POLISH✨

Iterate & Improve

Example: "Hor funny bana. Hor short kar. Emojis add kar. Tone hor warm kar."

Why: First output is never the best. The magic is in iteration. "Pehla draft kadey perfect nahi hunda — doosra try karo."

Section 04

Advanced Prompting Techniques

4 power moves that make your prompts professional-grade. Plus: build your personal Prompt Library.

TECHNIQUE 1🔗

Chain Prompting

"First do X, then based on that do Y." Break complex tasks into steps.

Example: "First, research the top 5 gyms in Ludhiana. Then, based on their weaknesses, create a marketing plan for a NEW gym."

TECHNIQUE 2🎭

Role Stacking

Give AI multiple expertise areas.

Example: "You are a marketing expert AND a Punjabi culture specialist AND a social media strategist. Create a Vaisakhi campaign for a clothing brand."

TECHNIQUE 3📊

Format Control

Tell AI exactly HOW to format the output.

Example: "Give answer as a table with columns: Idea, Cost, Revenue, Timeline" or "Bullet points only. Maximum 8 points."

TECHNIQUE 4🔄

Iteration Loops

Keep refining until it's perfect.

Example: "Now make it shorter. More formal. Add humor. Translate to Punjabi. Make the CTA stronger." First draft is never final.

AI Safety: Prompt Injection Warning

NEVER put personal info in prompts — Aadhaar, bank account, passwords. Show example of how AI can be manipulated. Always verify AI outputs with a second source for important decisions. "Kade vi personal jaankari AI nu nahi deni."
