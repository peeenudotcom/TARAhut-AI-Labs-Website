📖 My AI Storybook

📝 What You'll Do

Write a short story (6–10 pages) using ChatGPT to help with ideas and wording. Add AI-generated pictures using tools like Canva's AI!

🛠️ Tools to Use

ChatGPT (for story), Canva AI Image Generator (for pictures), Google Docs or Word (to put it all together)

🎯 What You'll Present

Read 2–3 pages of your story aloud. Show 3 of your AI images. Explain how you used AI to create it!

⭐ Why It's Great

Stories are fun to make AND to share! Your family will love hearing your story. Perfect if you love writing and art!

🎴 AI Greeting Card Collection

📝 What You'll Do

Design 5 beautiful greeting cards for different occasions: birthday, Eid, Diwali, New Year, and a friendship card!

🛠️ Tools to Use

Canva (design templates), ChatGPT (for the messages/poems inside each card), your own creativity!

🎯 What You'll Present

Show each card on screen, read the message inside, and explain which AI tool helped you make it!

⭐ Why It's Great

You'll actually USE your cards after — give them to family and friends! Perfect if you love design and making people happy!

📄 My School Report with AI

📝 What You'll Do

Pick any school subject (animals, space, history, science) and use AI to research and write a well-organised 1-page report!

🛠️ Tools to Use

Perplexity AI or ChatGPT (research), Gamma (to present it nicely), Canva (for visuals)

🎯 What You'll Present

Share 3 facts you learned, show your report, and explain how AI helped you learn this topic faster!

⭐ Why It's Great

You'll actually learn something new AND learn how to use AI for studying! Perfect if you're curious about a topic!

🖼️ AI Art Gallery

📝 What You'll Do

Create 6+ pieces of AI art using prompts you write yourself. Give each artwork a name and a short description!

🛠️ Tools to Use

Canva AI Image Generator, Adobe Firefly (free), or Bing Image Creator. Use Canva to display them in a gallery layout!

🎯 What You'll Present

Show your gallery, read the prompt you used for 3 artworks, and explain what you were trying to create!

⭐ Why It's Great

You can make STUNNING art even if you can't draw! Learning to write good prompts is a real AI skill! Perfect if you love creativity!
