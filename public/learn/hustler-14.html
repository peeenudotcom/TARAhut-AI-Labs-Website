Content Pillars — What to Post

Most businesses have no idea what to post on social media. You solve that with a content pillar strategy. 5 pillars = unlimited content ideas.

📚

The 5 Content Pillars

Every business's social media content falls into 5 categories. Mix these pillars and you never run out of ideas:
1. Educational — Tips, how-tos, industry knowledge
2. Promotional — Offers, sales, new products
3. Behind-the-Scenes — Team, process, daily life
4. Testimonials — Customer stories, reviews, results
5. Seasonal/Festival — Holiday greetings, seasonal offers

Ratio: 40% Educational, 20% Promo, 20% BTS, 10% Testimonials, 10% Seasonal.

📚

5 कंटेंट पिलर

हर बिज़नेस का सोशल मीडिया 5 कैटेगरी में आता है:
1. शिक्षाप्रद — टिप्स, गाइड
2. प्रोमोशनल — ऑफर, सेल
3. परदे का दृश्य — टीम, प्रक्रिया
4. प्रशंसापत्र — ग्राहक कहानियां
5. त्योहार/मौसम — बधाईयां, सीजनल ऑफर

EDUCATIONAL 40%📚

Tips & Knowledge

Position the business as an expert. "5 tips for healthy teeth" for a dentist. "How to choose the right hairstyle" for a salon. Most engaging content type. Builds trust and followers.

PROMOTIONAL 20%💸

Offers & Products

Direct selling: offers, new products, special deals. Keep to 20% — too much selling = unfollows. "Bechne se zyada value do. Log apne aap aayenge."

BEHIND-THE-SCENES 20%🎥

Human Side

Team photos, kitchen prep, making of products, daily operations. People connect with people, not logos. Most shareable content type. "Log insaanon se jdte hain, logos se nahi."

TESTIMONIALS 10%⭐

Social Proof

Customer stories, review screenshots, before/after results. Most persuasive content. "Dusron ki baat zyada impact karti hai."

SEASONAL 10%🎉

Festivals & Events

Diwali, Baisakhi, Eid, Independence Day. Pre-made greetings with business branding. High engagement, high sharing potential. "Festival posts = massive reach."

Platform-Specific Strategy

Not every business needs every platform. Match the right platform to the right business type for maximum impact with minimum effort.

INSTAGRAM📷

Visual Businesses

Best for: Restaurants, salons, boutiques, gyms, photography studios
Content: Photos, Reels, Stories, Carousels
Why: Visual-first platform. If the business looks good in photos, Instagram is #1. "Agar business dikhne mein acha hai, Instagram pe jaao."

FACEBOOK💻

Community Businesses

Best for: Coaching centers, events, local services, B2B
Content: Posts, Events, Groups, Marketplace
Why: Older demographic, community-focused. Great for local reach and events. "Facebook pe older audience hai jo decisions lete hain."

WHATSAPP💬

ALL Businesses

Best for: Every single business in India
Content: Status, Broadcast, Catalog, Direct messages
Why: 500M+ Indian users. Direct communication. Highest open rates. "WhatsApp pe sabse zyada log hain. Sabko chahiye."

GOOGLE MY BUSINESS🔍

Local Businesses

Best for: Any business with a physical location
Content: Posts, Photos, Offers, Updates
Why: Appears in Google Search and Maps. Free advertising. "Google pe dikhna hai toh GMB mandatory hai."

Platform Selection Exercise

For each business type, select the TOP 2 platforms:

  • Restaurant: Instagram (visual food content) + WhatsApp (orders/reservations)
  • Coaching Center: Facebook (community/events) + WhatsApp (parent communication)
  • Boutique: Instagram (fashion photos) + WhatsApp (direct sales)
  • Dental Clinic: Google My Business (local search) + Instagram (before/after)
