Indian Festival Marketing Calendar

India has a festival almost every week. Each festival is a marketing goldmine — special offers, themed content, festive greetings, seasonal promotions. Brands that align with festivals see 3-5x engagement spikes. Plan 30 days ahead for each major festival.

Major Festivals — Marketing Opportunities

JAN-MAR🪁

Lohri + Makar Sankranti + Holi

Lohri (Jan 13): Punjab's biggest winter festival. Makar Sankranti: pan-India. Republic Day (Jan 26). Valentine's Week (Feb). Holi (March): colors, sweets, fashion. Spring collections launch. Wedding season peaks. Each festival = themed content + offers.

APR-JUN🌾

Baisakhi + Eid + Summer

Baisakhi (Apr 13): Punjabi New Year, harvest festival. Eid al-Fitr: massive spending on clothes, food, gifts. Mother's Day (May). Summer sales. AC/cooler season. Ice cream, cold drinks promotions. School holidays = family business opportunities.

JUL-SEP🎋

Raksha Bandhan + Independence Day + Ganesh Chaturthi

Raksha Bandhan: gifts, sweets, fashion. Independence Day (Aug 15): patriotic content. Janmashtami. Ganesh Chaturthi: especially Maharashtra, South India. Teacher's Day. Navratri begins in September. Back-to-school season.

OCT-DEC🪔

Navratri + Dussehra + Diwali + Christmas

THE mega quarter. Navratri (9 days of content). Dussehra. Karwa Chauth. DIWALI: biggest shopping season in India. Black Friday equivalence. Christmas + New Year. Wedding season (Nov-Feb). This quarter = 40-50% of annual sales for many businesses.

Hindi Content Marketing Playbook

Hindi content is not just translation. It is about capturing the tone, emotion, and cultural rhythm that Hindi speakers respond to. The best Hindi marketing feels like a conversation with a friend, not a corporate message.

RULE 1💬

Conversational Tone

Use "aap" (respectful you) for older audiences, "tum" for younger. Avoid shuddh (pure) Hindi — nobody talks like that. Mix English words naturally: "order karo", "delivery available hai", "new collection launch ho gayi hai." Real-world spoken Hindi.

RULE 2🎭

Emotion Over Information

Hindi marketing is emotion-first. "Maa ke haathon ka pyaar, hamari mithai mein" hits harder than "Quality sweets available." Use storytelling, nostalgia, family bonds, pride, aspiration. Hindi audience responds to FEELING, then facts.

RULE 3🔤

Script Choice Matters

Devanagari for formal content (posters, ads, website). Roman/Latin for WhatsApp, SMS, casual social media. Always provide both when possible. WhatsApp broadcasts: Roman Hindi (more readable on phone). Instagram: Devanagari in image, Roman in caption.

RULE 4📱

Platform-Specific Hindi

WhatsApp: short, direct, Roman Hindi. Instagram: trendy Hinglish + Devanagari in graphics. Facebook: longer form, family-friendly, pure Hindi acceptable. YouTube: spoken Hindi (as natural as possible). Google Ads: both scripts for different keywords.

RULE 5⚡

Keywords in Hindi

People search in Hindi too! "Best mithai shop Amritsar" AND "sabse acchi mithai ki dukaan Amritsar." Target both. Google My Business description in Hindi + English. Website content in both. YouTube titles in Hindi get more clicks in tier-2 cities.

RULE 6🎯

CTAs in Hindi

"Abhi order karein" (Order now). "Aaj hi visit karein" (Visit today). "Humse sampark karein" (Contact us). "Limited offer — jaldi karein!" (Hurry!). Hindi CTAs convert better for Hindi-speaking audiences. Do not use English CTAs in Hindi content.

Section 05

Punjabi Content Marketing Playbook

Punjabi marketing is all about pride, warmth, and cultural identity. Punjab's business owners are proud of their language. Marketing in Punjabi creates instant trust and emotional connection. Food, fashion, agriculture, wedding — Punjabi content dominates these categories.

SECTOR🍲

Food & Restaurants

"Maa de haath da swaad" (taste of mother's hands). Food businesses in Punjab THRIVE on Punjabi content. Menu items in Punjabi, food descriptions that make you hungry, behind-the-scenes of tandoor cooking. Punjabi food culture is content gold.

SECTOR👗

Fashion & Boutiques

"Pind di kudi, shaher da style" (village girl, city style). Bridal wear, Phulkari, Patiala salwar — Punjabi fashion has identity. Content in Punjabi for boutiques connects with the target audience's pride. Wedding season content in Punjabi = viral potential.

SECTOR🌾

Agriculture & Equipment

Tractor dealers, seed companies, agricultural equipment. Farmers prefer Punjabi. "Navi fasal, nava nazariya" (new crop, new perspective). This is an underserved market — very few digital marketers create content for agricultural businesses in Punjabi.

SECTOR💒

Wedding Services

Photographers, decorators, caterers, venues, makeup artists. The Big Fat Punjabi Wedding is real. Wedding content in Punjabi gets massive engagement. "Tuhade viah nu yaadgaar banaun da vaada" (Promise to make your wedding memorable).

SECTOR🏥

Healthcare & Clinics

Patients trust doctors who communicate in their language. Health tips in Punjabi, dental care awareness, eye checkup reminders. "Apni sehat da khayal rakho" (Take care of your health). Builds community trust and appointment bookings.

SECTOR🏪

Retail & General Stores

Offers, new arrivals, festival specials in Punjabi. "Diwali te navi pedhkaar ayi hai!" (New generation has arrived for Diwali!). Local stores compete on personal touch — Punjabi content makes them feel like YOUR neighborhood shop, not a faceless brand.
