✨ Canva AI Workshop
Step-by-Step Guide

Canva is free, works in your browser, and has powerful AI features. Follow these steps on your phone or computer right now!

🚀

First: Set Up Canva (2 Minutes)

1. Go to canva.com on your phone or laptop.
2. Sign up with your Google account (free!).
3. You're in! Canva gives students extra free features. 🎉

✨

Feature 1: Magic Design

Click "Create a Design" → Type what you need in the search bar (like "School Sports Day Poster"). Canva's AI instantly generates multiple template options. Pick the one you like!

⭐ Best for: Quick starts when you don't know where to begin
🎨

Feature 2: Magic Write (AI Text)

Inside any design, click "Apps" → "Magic Write". Type what you need (like "Write a catchy headline for a Science Fair poster about volcanoes"). AI writes it for you!

⭐ Best for: Captions, headlines, and poster text you're stuck on
🖼️

Feature 3: Text to Image

Inside design → "Apps" → "Text to Image". Type: "A colorful infographic background with science equipment, bright colors, flat design." AI creates a custom image for your poster!

⭐ Best for: Unique images that are copyright-free and custom
📱

Feature 4: Social Media Templates

Choose "Instagram Post (Square)" or "Instagram Story". Browse thousands of templates. Click on any text to edit it. Change colors to match your school's colors. Download and post!

⭐ Best for: Event announcements, club posts, awareness campaigns
📊

Feature 5: Presentation AI

Click "Present" → "AI Presentation Maker". Type your topic (e.g., "Global Warming — 10 slides for Class 9 Science"). AI creates the entire presentation with content and design!

⭐ Best for: School presentations in under 5 minutes
💡

Pro Tip: The 3-Element Rule for Great Posters

Every great poster has exactly 3 focus elements:
1. One powerful headline — big, bold, easy to read in 2 seconds
2. One key visual — an image or graphic that tells the story
3. One clear action — what should the reader DO? (Join us! Register here! Come and see!)

Instagram Post Format Tips

Square (1:1): 1080×1080px — works best in the feed
Story (9:16): 1080×1920px — for full-screen stories
Reel Cover: Same as story size
Font rule: Maximum 2 fonts per design. Title = bold. Body = regular.
Color rule: 2-3 colors max. Use your school colors for school events!
