When someone searches "restaurant near me" on Google, GMB results appear first. If a business is not on GMB, it is invisible. You will fix that.

GMB Profile Setup — What Every Business Needs

ESSENTIAL🏪

Business Name & Category

Exact business name as it appears on signage. Choose the most specific category (e.g., "Punjabi Restaurant" not just "Restaurant"). Category determines which searches you appear in.

ESSENTIAL📍

Address & Service Area

Full address with landmark for map accuracy. Service area if they do delivery/home visits. Pin location must be exact — wrong pin = lost customers. "Pin sahi jagah pe lagao."

ESSENTIAL📞

Phone, Hours & Website

Primary phone number (WhatsApp-enabled preferred). Accurate business hours including holidays. Website link (even if just Instagram page). "Galat hours = angry customers."

HIGH IMPACT📷

Photos & Videos

Minimum 10 high-quality photos: storefront, interior, products/menu, team. Use Canva to create professional photo frames. Businesses with photos get 42% more direction requests. "Photos = trust."

HIGH IMPACT📝

Business Description

750 characters maximum. Use ChatGPT with 5-Block Framework to write it. Include: what you do, years of experience, specialties, location keywords, CTA. "Yeh free advertisement hai."

ONGOING⭐

Reviews & Responses

Encourage customers to leave reviews. Respond to EVERY review (positive and negative). ChatGPT can generate professional response templates. Reviews = social proof = more customers.

GMB Posts & Local SEO

GMB is not set-and-forget. Weekly posts, updated photos, and responses keep the profile active and visible. Active profiles rank higher in local search.

5 Types of GMB Posts

TYPE 1📣

What's New

General updates about the business. New menu items, new staff, expanded hours, new location. "Naya kya hai batao."

TYPE 2🎁

Offer / Promotion

Time-limited discounts and deals with start/end dates. "20% off this weekend" drives immediate foot traffic. Most effective GMB post type.

TYPE 3📅

Event

Upcoming events: grand opening, live music night, seasonal sale. Includes date, time, and details. Creates urgency and excitement.

TYPE 4📸

Product / Service

Highlight specific products or services with photos and pricing. "Today's special: Butter Chicken ₹350." Visual and specific.

TYPE 5💬

COVID/Safety Update

Health and safety measures, sanitization practices. Builds trust. "Aapki safety humari priority."

Local SEO Basics

GMB is the foundation of local SEO. Key tips:
1. Use city + business type in description (e.g., "best dentist in Jalandhar")
2. Add photos with descriptive filenames (e.g., jalandhar-dental-clinic-reception.jpg)
3. Encourage reviews with city names ("Great dental clinic in Jalandhar!")
4. Post weekly to signal activity to Google
5. Respond to all reviews within 24 hours

WhatsApp Business — The Communication Engine

500 million+ WhatsApp users in India. Every customer is on WhatsApp. Setting up WhatsApp Business properly is a ₹3,000-5,000 service.

WhatsApp Business Features to Set Up

PROFILE👤

Business Profile

Professional photo (logo or storefront), business description, address, hours, email, website. This is the first thing customers see. Make it professional. "Pehli impression professional honi chahiye."

CATALOG🛒

Product Catalog

Add products/services with images, descriptions, and prices. Customers browse your catalog directly in WhatsApp. Like a mini-website inside WhatsApp. Design catalog images in Canva.

QUICK REPLIES⚡

Quick Reply Templates

Pre-set responses for common questions: greeting, price inquiry, hours, location, booking confirmation, thank you, review request. Saves 80% of response time. "10 quick replies set karo."

LABELS🏷️

Contact Labels

Organize contacts: New Lead, Active Client, Past Client, Hot Lead, Follow-Up. Color-coded for easy management. Simple CRM inside WhatsApp. "Labels = organized business."

BROADCAST📣

Broadcast Lists

Send offers to up to 256 contacts at once. Recipients see it as a personal message, not a group. Best practice: max 1-2 broadcasts per week. "Broadcast = free marketing channel."

STATUS📷

WhatsApp Status

Daily posts visible for 24 hours. Behind-the-scenes, daily offers, new products. Think of it as free Stories. Post at least once daily. "Roz ek status post karo."
