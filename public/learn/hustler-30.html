Section 02 — Contact Sorting

Categorize Your Contacts

Every contact from Days 28-29 falls into one of three categories. Your strategy is different for each. Sort them now.

HOT🔥

Said "Yes" or "Send Details"

Action: Call within the hour. Send proposal. Schedule a 15-minute meeting TODAY. These are your priority. Drop everything for a HOT contact.

WARM🌡️

Said "Sochke Batata Hoon"

Action: Send the free sample post FIRST. Then message: "Ji aapke liye yeh sample banaaya. Kaisa laga? Agar aur chahiye toh baat karte hain." Convert WARM to HOT with proof of quality.

COLD❄️

Polite But Not Engaged

Action: Add to WhatsApp broadcast list for future nurture. Do NOT chase today. They may come back in 2-3 months when they see your work. Low priority today.

Phone Call Script for HOT Contacts

"Namaste [Name] ji, main [Your Name] bol raha hoon. Kal/parson aapki dukaan pe aaya tha. Aapne interest dikhaya tha digital marketing mein. Maine aapke liye ek plan ready kiya hai. Kya main aaj aa ke 15 minute mein samjha doon? Aapke liye 11 baje ya 3 baje, kab theek rahega?"

The Two-Option Close

Never ask "kab milein?" (open-ended). Always give two options: "11 baje ya 3 baje?" This is called the "alternative close" -- it assumes the meeting will happen and only asks about timing. Much higher conversion than open-ended questions.

Section 04 — Proposals

Proposal Customization Sprint

For each HOT contact, customize your proposal template in 10 minutes. Business name, specific services, sample attached, clear pricing, strong call to action.

Proposal Customization Checklist (10 Min Per Proposal)

  • Business name and owner name inserted everywhere (not generic)
  • Specific services recommended based on YOUR conversation with them
  • Sample deliverable attached or mentioned (the free sample post)
  • Clear pricing -- recommend the middle tier package
  • Call to action: "Kya main kal aake 15 minute mein sab samjha doon?"

AI Prompt: Generate Proposal for Specific Business

Click to copy: "Create a 1-page digital marketing proposal for [Business Name], a [business type] in [city]. Include: (1) Current status: no social media presence, no Google listing. (2) Recommended services: Instagram setup + 12 posts/month + Google My Business. (3) Pricing: ₹5,000/month (Growth Package). (4) Timeline: setup in 3 days, first posts within 1 week. (5) What they get: 12 designed posts, 4 reels scripts, GMB optimization, monthly report. Keep it professional, clear, and in Hindi-English mix."
