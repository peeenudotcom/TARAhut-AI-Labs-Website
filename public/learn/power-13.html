Upload any data file to ChatGPT and ask questions:

Section 03

Survey Creation & Competitive Analysis

Create professional surveys with AI. Analyze your competition. Know your market better than anyone.

AI Survey Creator

Create professional surveys in seconds:

📋 Create a 10-question customer satisfaction survey for a coaching centre in Punjab. Include: 3 rating scale questions (1-5), 3 multiple choice questions, 2 yes/no questions, and 2 open-ended questions. Focus on teaching quality, facilities, and value for money. Format so I can copy directly into Google Forms.

Then use AI to analyze the responses too!

Competitive Analysis Framework

STEP 1🔍

Identify Competitors

"List my top 5 competitors for [business] in [location]" — AI maps your competitive landscape instantly.

STEP 2📊

Compare Them

"Compare them on: price, quality, services, online presence, reviews" — Side-by-side analysis in table format.

STEP 3🎯

Find Your Edge

"What are their weaknesses I can exploit? What unique positioning should I take?" — Your strategic advantage.

Full Competitive Analysis

Complete competitive intelligence in one prompt:

📋 I want to start an AI training institute in Mohali/Chandigarh. Do a competitive analysis: 1) List top 5 competitors with their courses, pricing, batch size, and reviews 2) Compare them in a table 3) Identify their weaknesses 4) Suggest my unique positioning that would make me stand out. Be specific to Punjab market.

Same framework works for freelancing, job search, or any business idea.
