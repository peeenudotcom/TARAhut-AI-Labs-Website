Facebook Business Page Setup & Optimization

A Facebook Business Page is NOT the same as a personal profile. Most Indian businesses either use their personal profile for business (wrong!) or have an unclaimed, incomplete page. Set up a proper page — it unlocks Meta Business Suite, ads, insights, and scheduling.

SETUP 1📄

Create Business Page

facebook.com/pages/create. Choose category (Local Business, Restaurant, Professional Service, etc.). Add business name, bio, address, phone, website, hours. Upload high-res cover photo (820x312px) and profile picture (logo, 170x170px).

SETUP 2📝

About Section

Write a keyword-rich "About" section. Include what you do, where you are, what makes you different, and contact info. Maximum 255 characters for the short description — make every word count. This is what appears in search.

SETUP 3🔘

CTA Button

Choose the right Call-to-Action button: "Call Now" for services, "Shop Now" for products, "Book Now" for appointments, "Send Message" for inquiries. This button gets more clicks than any post. Test different CTAs monthly.

SETUP 4📌

Page Tabs + Templates

Customize tab order: Home, Posts, Reviews, Services, Shop, Videos. Remove unused tabs (Notes, Events if not needed). Choose page template: Services, Shopping, Restaurants, etc. — each has different default tabs and layouts.

SETUP 5💬

Messaging Setup

Enable Messenger for the page. Set up instant reply: "Thank you for messaging [Business Name]! Hum 1 ghante mein reply karenge." Set up FAQs that customers can tap. Away message for after hours.

SETUP 6🔒

Page Roles & Access

Add client as Admin, yourself as Editor or Admin. NEVER keep only one admin — if that account gets locked, the page is lost forever. Set up 2FA on all admin accounts. Document access details for client handover.

Instagram Business Account Setup

Instagram is the visual powerhouse. Business accounts unlock insights, contact buttons, shopping, and the ability to schedule through Meta Business Suite. Instagram is where 18-35 year olds discover brands in India.

STEP 1🔄

Switch to Business Account

Settings > Account > Switch to Professional Account > Business. Link to Facebook Page (required for Meta Business Suite). Choose business category. This takes 30 seconds but unlocks everything.

STEP 2📝

Optimize Bio

150 characters maximum. Format: What you do | Where you are | CTA. Use line breaks for readability. Include 1-2 relevant hashtags. Add link (use Linktree for multiple links). Bio is the only place for a clickable link.

STEP 3🔘

Contact Buttons

Add Call, Email, Directions buttons. These appear prominently on the profile. For food businesses: add Order Food button. For salons: add Book Now. Each button type drives specific actions. Choose what matches the business goal.

STEP 4💡

Story Highlights

Create branded highlight covers using Canva. Essential highlights: Menu/Services, Reviews, Location, FAQ, Behind-the-Scenes. Highlights are the first thing people explore after the bio. They are your mini-website on Instagram.

STEP 5🎨

Grid Aesthetic

Plan the first 9 posts (the grid that shows on profile). Alternate between product shots, customer testimonials, behind-the-scenes, and branded quotes. Use consistent color palette and fonts. First impression = follow or scroll past.

STEP 6🔗

Cross-Platform Linking

Link Instagram to Facebook Page. Enable cross-posting (optional). Link to WhatsApp Business (the WhatsApp button on Instagram). Now all three platforms are connected. One ecosystem, three touchpoints with customers.

Insights & Analytics Dashboard

Meta Business Suite Insights show exactly what is working and what is not. Reach, engagement, follower demographics, best-performing posts. You use data to make decisions, not guesswork. This is what separates amateurs from professionals.

METRIC👁️

Reach

How many UNIQUE people saw your content. Higher reach = more people exposed to the brand. Track weekly. If reach drops, algorithm is not favoring your content. Fix: more engagement-worthy posts, reels, carousels. Reach is your awareness metric.

METRIC💬

Engagement Rate

Likes + comments + shares + saves divided by reach. Industry average: 1-3%. Above 5% is excellent. Saves and shares matter MORE than likes. A post with 10 saves is better than a post with 100 likes. Track engagement rate, not vanity metrics.

METRIC👥

Follower Growth

Net new followers per week/month. Track growth rate, not absolute number. Losing followers? Check if content quality dropped or if you posted something off-brand. Consistent growth of 2-5% monthly is healthy for local businesses.

METRIC📊

Top Performing Posts

Identify which posts got the most reach and engagement. Replicate the pattern. Was it a reel? A testimonial? A behind-the-scenes? Double down on what works. This is data-driven content strategy, not guessing what people want.

METRIC🕐

Active Times

When your audience is online. Meta shows hourly and daily breakdowns. Schedule posts 30 minutes BEFORE the peak time. Different audiences have different active times. Check this monthly — patterns change with seasons.

METRIC🎯

Demographics

Age, gender, city breakdown of your followers. Tailor content to your actual audience, not assumed audience. A salon might think they target women 20-30, but data shows most followers are 35-45. Adapt content accordingly.

Ads Manager Fundamentals

Meta Ads reach billions. Even ₹100/day budgets can drive real results for local businesses. You do not need to be an ads expert — you need to know the basics to run simple, effective local campaigns. This is an upsell service on top of organic management.

3 levels. Campaign = what you want. Ad Set = who sees it. Ad = what they see.
OBJECTIVE👁️

Awareness Campaigns

Goal: maximum reach. Best for new businesses, grand openings, event announcements. Show your ad to the most people in your target area. Budget: ₹200-500/day. Run for 7-14 days. Measures: reach, impressions, CPM (cost per 1000 views).

OBJECTIVE🔗

Traffic Campaigns

Goal: drive clicks to website or WhatsApp. Best for businesses with websites, menus, or booking pages. People click your ad and land on your page. Budget: ₹300-500/day. Measures: clicks, CTR (click-through rate), CPC (cost per click).

OBJECTIVE📩

Lead Generation

Goal: collect contact information. Meta's built-in lead form — user fills out name, phone, email without leaving Facebook/Instagram. Perfect for service businesses (clinics, coaching, real estate). Budget: ₹500-1000/day for quality leads.

TARGETING📍

Local Targeting

Target by: city, pin code, radius (1-50 km from location). Layer with: age, gender, interests, behaviors. Example: "Women, 25-45, within 5km of Amritsar, interested in beauty and wellness." Hyper-local targeting is your superpower for local clients.

Start Small, Prove ROI, Then Scale

Never promise huge results from ads immediately. Start with ₹3,000-5,000 total ad spend for a test campaign (7-14 days). Show the client the results. If it works, scale up. Your management fee: ₹2,000-5,000/month on top of ad spend. Never mix your fee with ad spend.
