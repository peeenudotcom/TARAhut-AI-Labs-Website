What NOT to do
Every professional prompt has all 5 blocks. Missing even 1 degrades output quality significantly.
BLOCK 1👤

ROLE

Tell AI WHO it is. "You are a professional marketing copywriter for small businesses in Punjab." This sets the expertise level, tone, and perspective. Without a role, AI gives generic output.

BLOCK 2📋

CONTEXT

Give background information. "The client is a newly opened gym in Jalandhar targeting college students aged 18-25." More context = more relevant output. AI can't guess your client's situation.

BLOCK 3🎯

TASK

What EXACTLY should AI do? "Write 5 Instagram captions for their launch week." Be specific. "Write something" vs "Write 5 Instagram captions" — massive difference in output.

BLOCK 4📐

FORMAT

How should the output look? "Each caption: max 150 words, include 1 emoji, end with a CTA, include 5 hashtags." Format instructions prevent AI from rambling or giving wrong structure.

BLOCK 5🚫

CONSTRAINTS

What should AI AVOID? "Tone: energetic and young. Language: Hinglish. Do not mention competitor gyms. No cliches." Constraints prevent common AI mistakes and keep output on-brand.

🧠

Memory Trick: RCTFC

Raju Chai Tandoori Fresh Chaat — Remember this and you'll never forget the 5 blocks! Or make your own: Real Content That Feels Commercial. "RCTFC = Raju Chai Tandoori Fresh Chaat. Yaad rakhna easy hai!"

The 5-Block Framework is now in your brain. This single framework will generate thousands of rupees for you.
