💡 Presentation Do's and Don'ts

✅ DO

Speak slowly and clearly 🗣️
Look at the audience 👀
Smile at the start and end 😊
Take a deep breath if nervous 🌬️

❌ DON'T

Read off the screen 📺
Speak too fast 💨
Keep your back to people 🚶
Say sorry if you make a mistake 🙊
