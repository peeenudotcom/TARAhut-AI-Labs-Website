Video Script Mastery

A great video starts with a great script. Learn the formula for writing scripts that convert viewers into customers. The script determines 80% of the video's effectiveness.

📝 The HOOK-VALUE-CTA Script Formula
🪝
HOOK
First 5 seconds
→
💰
VALUE
Next 20-40 seconds
→
👉
CTA
Last 5-10 seconds
HOOK grabs attention. VALUE delivers the message. CTA drives action. Every video must follow this structure. "Pehle dhyan pakdo, phir value do, phir action bolo."
HOOK🪝

Grab Attention in 5 Seconds

Start with a question, surprising fact, or direct address. Examples: "Kya aap jaante hain Jalandhar ka sabse modern dental clinic kaunsa hai?" or "Tired of bad teeth? We have the solution." If they don't stop scrolling in 5 seconds, the video fails.

VALUE💰

Deliver the Message (20-40s)

Introduce the business. Highlight 2-3 key benefits (not features). Make it about the CUSTOMER, not the business. "Aapki smile hamari zimmedari" beats "Hamare paas latest machines hain."

CTA👉

Drive Action (5-10s)

Tell them EXACTLY what to do: "Call 98765-XXXXX", "Visit us at Main Road", "DM us on Instagram", "WhatsApp pe message karo." One clear action. Not three. "Ek CTA. Sirf ek."
