Section 01

What's a Prompt? ⚡

A prompt is YOUR instruction to AI — like placing an order at a restaurant! The better your order, the better your food! 🍽️

🧙‍♂️

A Prompt is Like a Magic Spell!

When you type something to AI, that's called a prompt. A vague prompt gives a boring answer. A detailed, clear prompt gives an amazing answer — like a magic spell that gets exactly what you want! ✨

🍽️ The Food Ordering Analogy

😕 Bad Order

"Give me food"
What do you get? Who knows! Maybe just plain rice... 😐
VS

😍 Great Order

"Give me hot butter naan with paneer, extra spicy, and a glass of lassi"
You get exactly what you wanted! 🎉 Delicious!
💡

Same Rule for AI!

"Write a story" → boring, generic story 😴
"Write a funny adventure story about a Class 6 student from Kotkapura who finds a magic pencil" → amazing, specific, exciting story! 🎉

📖

The Prompt Recipe Formula!

Every great prompt has these ingredients:

WHAT + WHO + HOW + EXTRA DETAILS = 🌟 Amazing Answer!

Example: "Write (WHAT) a funny poem (HOW) for a 10-year-old (WHO) about school homework (EXTRA DETAILS)"

Section 02

Good Prompt vs Bad Prompt! ✨

See the HUGE difference a good prompt makes! Three real examples for you.

📝 Example 1: Homework Help

❌ Bad Prompt
"Help me with homework"
AI doesn't know what subject, what topic, or what kind of help!
✅ Good Prompt
"I am a Class 6 student. Explain the Water Cycle in 5 simple sentences with a drawing description"
AI knows exactly what to do! 🎯

📖 Example 2: Writing a Story

❌ Bad Prompt
"Write a story"
You'll get a random, boring story with no fun!
✅ Good Prompt
"Write a funny 10-line story about a Class 5 student named Riya from Kotkapura who discovers her cat can talk"
Now THAT is exciting! 🐱✨

🎨 Example 3: Making Art

❌ Bad Prompt
"Draw something"
What will it draw? Nobody knows... 🤷
✅ Good Prompt
"A colourful cartoon of a lion wearing a yellow turban, standing in front of a golden wheat field in Punjab, sunshine"
Beautiful and specific! 🦁👑

💡 The 5 Prompt Power Rules!

1. 🎯 Be specific — say exactly what you want
2. 👤 Say who — "for a Class 6 student" helps AI match your level
3. 📏 Say how long — "in 5 sentences" or "in 100 words"
4. 🎨 Add style — "funny", "simple", "exciting", "detailed"
5. ➕ Add details — names, places, topics you care about!
