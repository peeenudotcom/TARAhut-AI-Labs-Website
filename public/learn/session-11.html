How to Generate Your First Voiceover

▶️

Step-by-Step: First Voiceover in 5 Minutes

1. Go to elevenlabs.io — sign in with Google (free: 10,000 characters/month)
2. Click "Text to Speech" from the left panel
3. In the Voice Selector, try: "Rachel" (professional female) or "Adam" (professional male) or search "Hindi" for Indian voices
4. Paste your text in the text box. Keep sentences clear and naturally punctuated
5. Adjust settings: Stability (higher = consistent), Clarity + Similarity (higher = more human-like), Style Exaggeration (for emotion)
6. Click "Generate" → Play → Download as MP3
7. Use this audio in CapCut, HeyGen, or any video editor

💡

Pro Tip: Writing for Voice

Good TTS starts with good text. Write conversationally: short sentences, no jargon, commas where you'd pause. Avoid abbreviations — spell them out (say "rupees" not "Rs."). Use punctuation strategically — a comma creates a small pause, a period creates a longer one. Test with 2–3 sentences first before generating long audio.

What is Audio Branding?

Social Media Content Machine

The complete end-to-end workflow: from topic idea to published Reel in under 30 minutes. One workflow, infinite content.

⚡

The 5-Step Content Machine

Every piece of social media content follows the same pipeline. Once you master this flow, you can produce 2–3 posts per day, for any business, consistently.

Step 1: Research the Topic (Perplexity — 5 min)

🔬

Find What's Worth Posting About

Ask Perplexity: "What are the 5 most searched questions about [topic/business type] in India right now?" or "What are trending topics for [restaurant/clinic/fashion] Instagram accounts this week?"

Pick one topic from the results. A post answering a real question always performs better than random promotional content. You're not creating content — you're answering what people are already asking.

Step 2: Write the Caption (Claude — 5 min)

📄

Caption + Hook + Hashtags in One Prompt

Prompt Claude: "Write an Instagram caption for [business type] about [topic]. Include: 1 strong hook in the first line (something that makes people stop scrolling), 3–5 bullet points of value, a call to action to WhatsApp at [number], and 10 relevant hashtags. Tone: [friendly / professional / informative]. Max 150 words."

Edit the result for local context (mention Kotkapura, Faridkot, Punjab where relevant). Add emojis to match the brand voice.

Step 3: Generate the Visual (Ideogram or Canva — 5 min)

🎨

Matching Visual for the Caption

For image posts: Use Ideogram (for text-heavy graphics) or Canva Magic Media (for product/atmosphere images). The visual should reinforce the caption's message.
For Reels: Shoot 30 seconds of raw video on your phone OR use a Canva Reel template with the AI image as background. Fast, professional, consistent.

Step 4: Create the Reel (CapCut — 10 min)

📸

Turn the Visual into a Short Video

Import your image or video clip into CapCut. Add: auto-captions, background music (from CapCut library — soft and non-distracting), a text overlay with your key message in the first 3 seconds, your logo watermark, and a call-to-action text at the end. Export at 1080p, 9:16 (vertical for Reels).

Step 5: Add Voiceover (ElevenLabs — 5 min)

🎤

Professional Voice, Not Your Shaky Speaking Voice

Take the key points from your caption. Write a 20–30 second voiceover script (bullet points, not full paragraphs). Generate in ElevenLabs in your brand's chosen voice. Import the MP3 into CapCut and layer it over the video. Lower background music volume. The result: a professional narrated Reel — no camera, no microphone, no nervousness.

The Rule of 3 Content Types

Professional social media managers follow the 3-1-1 rule:
3 Value posts — tips, education, how-to (builds trust and following)
1 Social proof post — testimonial, before/after, review (builds credibility)
1 Promotional post — offer, discount, service highlight (drives sales)

Never post 3 promotional posts in a row. Lead with value, earn attention, then offer.
