Custom GPTs: आपके AI Products

Custom GPTs: ਤੁਹਾਡੇ AI Products

Custom GPTs are permanent AI applications built inside ChatGPT. They combine system prompts, knowledge files, and optional actions into a shareable tool anyone can use. Think of them as apps built without code. "Custom GPTs = bina code de AI apps."

Instructions + Knowledge + Actions = Custom GPT. It is an AI app that anyone with the link can use.
Component📜

Instructions (System Prompt)

The core of your GPT. Everything from Session 8 applies: Identity, Rules, Constraints, Output Format. But now it is permanent — saved inside the GPT, not typed every time. Max ~8000 characters. This is where all your persona work pays off. "Session 8 da system prompt = GPT di jaan."

Component📚

Knowledge Files

Upload PDFs, CSVs, text files, and documents. The GPT uses these as its reference library. Up to 20 files, 512MB total. Upload your SOPs, price lists, product catalogs, FAQs, and policy documents. The GPT will cite these when answering. "Apne business de documents upload karo — GPT ohna ton jawab dega."

Component⚙️

Actions (API Integrations)

Connect your GPT to external services via APIs. Check inventory, send emails, look up order status, or query your database. Actions turn your GPT from a chatbot into a functional business tool. Requires OpenAPI spec. Advanced feature for developers. "APIs naal connect karo — GPT real kaam karega."

Component🔗

Conversation Starters

Pre-written prompts that appear when someone opens your GPT. Design them to showcase the GPT's best capabilities. "What is my order status?", "Help me write a proposal", "Analyze my sales data." First impression matters. "Pehla impression — conversation starters naal banaao."

"Meri team nu har vaari same sawaal answer karne pende ne." Build a GPT with your FAQ document uploaded. Share the link with your team or customers. They get instant, accurate answers 24/7. You reclaim hours every week. This is the ROI of Custom GPTs.

Claude Projects: Knowledge Workspaces

Claude Projects: Knowledge Workspaces

Claude Projects offer something Custom GPTs cannot: massive knowledge bases (200K tokens), separate workspaces, and superior reasoning for complex tasks. "Claude Projects = GPTs da advanced version."

🤖

Custom GPTs (ChatGPT)

Max 20 files/512MB. Shareable via link. GPT Store distribution. Actions for API integration. Great for simple, shareable tools. Larger user base. Better for customer-facing tools.

Best for: Sharing & Simple Tools
🔮

Claude Projects

200K token context. Upload entire codebases, long documents, and complex reference materials. Superior reasoning for analysis. Better for internal knowledge-heavy workspaces. Stronger at nuanced analysis.

When to Use GPT vs Claude Project

Use Custom GPT when: sharing with customers, building simple tools, wanting API actions, or publishing to GPT Store. Use Claude Project when: working with large document sets, needing deep analysis, internal team use, or complex reasoning tasks. Many businesses use BOTH: GPTs for external and Claude Projects for internal. "Bahar layi GPT, andar layi Claude."

You now know how to build on both platforms. Choose based on your use case.

Section 04

Deploy, Share, & Iterate

Deploy, Share, & Iterate

Deploy, Share, & Iterate

Building is half the work. Deploying to users and iterating based on feedback is what makes AI tools valuable. "Banana adha kaam hai — deploy karna te improve karna asli kaam hai."

Step 1🔨

Internal Testing

Before sharing, test with 20 diverse queries: normal questions, edge cases, out-of-scope requests, hostile inputs. Use the 10-point stress test from Session 8. Fix every failure before deploying. "20 test queries — ek vi fail na hove."

Step 2👥

Beta Users

Share with 3-5 trusted team members or customers. Ask them to use it for a week and report: What worked well? What was confusing? What answers were wrong? Collect feedback in a simple spreadsheet. "3-5 beta users — honest feedback lo."

Step 3🛠

Iterate Instructions

Based on beta feedback, update the system prompt. Most common fixes: adding constraints for edge cases, improving output format, adding knowledge files for common questions the GPT could not answer. "Feedback de basis te improve karo."

Step 4🔗

Share & Scale

Custom GPT: Share via link (Only me, With link, or Public/GPT Store). Claude Project: Invite team members. Start with "With link" sharing, monitor usage, then consider GPT Store for public tools. "Pehla link naal share karo, phir scale karo."
