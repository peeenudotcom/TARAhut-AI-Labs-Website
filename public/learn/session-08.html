Gamma AI — Presentations in Seconds

Gamma turns text into beautiful, professional presentations. What used to take hours in PowerPoint now takes minutes. No design skills required.

🌟

The Problem Gamma Solves

PowerPoint is powerful but slow. You spend more time formatting than thinking. Gamma inverts this: you focus on the content, and Gamma handles all design decisions — layouts, colors, fonts, spacing, imagery. The result looks like it was made by a professional designer.

Gamma's Core Features

The Magic Feature⚡

Text to Presentation

Type a topic or paste your outline and Gamma generates a complete, beautifully designed presentation — structure, content, visuals, and formatting — in under 60 seconds. Choose the number of slides and Gamma does the rest.

Design Quality🎨

Professional Templates

Gamma's templates are genuinely beautiful — modern, clean, professional. Choose from dozens of themes sorted by purpose: pitch deck, business report, educational, creative. Each template is fully editable after generation.

Smart AI🤖

AI-Powered Layouts

Gamma's AI doesn't just apply a template — it intelligently decides the best layout for each slide based on your content. A comparison slide gets a side-by-side layout. A list of features gets a card grid. The AI makes design decisions for you.

Live Sharing🌐

Present & Share Online

Every Gamma presentation gets a live link you can share instantly. Recipients view it in a browser — no PowerPoint needed. You can also export to PDF or PowerPoint format if required.

Flexibility✏️

Full Editability

After Gamma generates the presentation, you have full control: edit text, swap images, change layouts, add slides, reorder content. The AI creates the starting point, you refine it to perfection.

Free Tier💰

Pricing

Free plan: 400 AI credits (enough for ~10-15 presentations). Paid plans from $8/month for unlimited. For most students and professionals, the free tier is more than sufficient to start.

How to Create a Presentation in Gamma

Step 1: Go to gamma.app → Click "Create New" → Choose "Generate with AI" Step 2: Choose your starting point: - "Generate from text" (paste your outline or notes) - "Generate from prompt" (just describe what you want) - "Import from file" (upload a Word/PDF document) Step 3: Write your prompt (example): "Create a 10-slide professional pitch deck for a cloud kitchen business in Kotkapura, Punjab. Include: Problem/Opportunity, Solution, Market Size, Business Model, Financial Projections, Team, and Call to Action. Tone: Professional, confident, investor-ready." Step 4: Choose theme and number of slides → Click Generate → Review → Edit → Share

60-Second Action

Go to gamma.app and create your first presentation right now:

  • Click "Generate with AI"
  • Choose "Generate from prompt"
  • Type: "Introduction to [your name] — who I am, what I do, and my goals for 2026. 6 slides."
  • Watch a presentation appear in under 60 seconds
You just built a presentation faster than most people take to open PowerPoint.

Canva AI — Your Design Powerhouse

Canva was already the world's most popular design tool. With AI, it became something else entirely. Here's everything you need to know.

🔥

Canva AI is Not Just One Feature

Canva's AI isn't a single feature — it's an entire ecosystem of AI tools woven into the design platform. Magic Design, Magic Edit, Text to Image, Background Remover, Magic Resize, Bulk Create, Brand Kit — each one replaces hours of manual work. Used together, they make a professional designer-level output achievable by anyone.

Canva's Most Powerful AI Features

Magic Feature💡

Magic Design

Upload your content (text, images, logo) and Magic Design instantly creates multiple fully-designed templates customized to your content. No starting from scratch — AI designs first, you tweak. Perfect for social media posts, presentations, flyers.

Generate Images🎨

Text to Image

Type a description and Canva generates custom images within your design. No need to search stock photos or pay for photography. "A warm, inviting interior of a Punjabi dhaba at sunset" generates exactly that, in your design's style.

Brand Control🌟

Brand Kit

Upload your logo, colors, and fonts once. Canva applies them to any design automatically. Every social post, presentation, and flyer is automatically on-brand. For businesses, this is invaluable — brand consistency at zero effort.

Scale📊

Bulk Create

Upload a spreadsheet with data (names, products, prices, cities) and Canva generates hundreds of personalized designs at once. Example: 500 personalized certificates from a single template + Excel sheet. What took days takes minutes.

Marketing📷

Social Media Templates

1000s of AI-enhanced templates for every platform: Instagram posts, Stories, Reels covers, LinkedIn banners, YouTube thumbnails, WhatsApp Status graphics. Click, customize, download. Consistent branding across all channels.

Editing✂️

Magic Edit & Background Remover

Magic Edit: select any element in an image and tell AI what to replace it with. Background Remover: removes image backgrounds instantly — no Photoshop needed. These two features alone replace ₹500/hour photo editing work.

A small business owner in our last batch was spending ₹8,000/month on a freelance designer for social media posts. After learning Canva AI in one session, she created an entire month's content in an afternoon — with better brand consistency than before. That's ₹96,000/year in savings, plus creative control. The tool pays for itself in the first week.

60-Second Action

If you don't have Canva yet, sign up free at canva.com. Then:

  • Create a new design → choose "Instagram Post"
  • Click "Magic Design" and upload a photo or type your topic
  • Browse the AI-generated designs and pick one to customize
  • Try "Text to Image" to generate a background image from a description
Canva AI is now in your toolkit. This is your design team for life.

Client proposals & reports✅ Excellent structure & sharing⚠ Can be done but slowerGamma
AI image generation in design⚠ Limited image customization✅ Text to Image + Magic EditCanva

The result: a presentation that looks like a top-tier agency created it — because technically, two AI-powered design tools did.

📋 Client Brief

You've been hired by Tandoor & Tales — a new family restaurant in Kotkapura, Punjab specializing in authentic Punjabi cuisine. They need a 10-slide investor pitch deck to raise ₹30 lakhs for expansion. They also need social media launch content for Instagram (5 posts). Budget is tight. Timeline: 48 hours.

What's your approach? Click to see the professional recommendation:

⚠

Gamma Only — Partial Solution

Gamma handles the pitch deck perfectly. But for Instagram social media content, Gamma isn't the right tool — it's built for presentations and documents, not social media graphics. You'd need to create the social posts elsewhere. This approach solves 60% of the brief.

⚠

Canva Only — Also Partial

Canva creates excellent social media content. You can also build a presentation in Canva, but it takes much longer than Gamma and the AI isn't optimized for pitch deck structure. You'd spend 3-4 hours on the presentation alone. This approach solves 70% of the brief, but isn't efficient.

✅

Professional Approach: Both Tools — Strategically

Step 1 (45 min): Use Canva AI for brand assets + social posts. Step 2 (20 min): Use Gamma for the pitch deck. Bring Canva assets into Gamma.

Step 1: Canva AI — Social Media Posts

Canva Magic Design prompt: "Create 5 Instagram posts for Tandoor & Tales restaurant launch. Brand: warm, authentic Punjabi hospitality. Orange, deep red, golden colors. Post 1: Grand opening announcement Post 2: Signature dish highlight (Dal Makhani) Post 3: Chef & team introduction Post 4: Ambiance & interior reveal Post 5: Special launch week offer" Then use Text to Image for custom food photography: "A bowl of rich, creamy Dal Makhani in a copper karahi, warm lighting, authentic Punjabi kitchen background, professional food photography style"

Step 2: Gamma AI — 10-Slide Pitch Deck

Slide 01
Cover: Tandoor & Tales

Brand logo, tagline, location, investor pitch context

Slide 02
The Opportunity

Kotkapura dining gap, growing middle class, food delivery boom

Slide 03
Our Restaurant

Concept, authenticity story, menu highlights

Slide 04
Market Size

Punjab food service market, growth projections, our slice

Slide 05
Business Model

Revenue streams: dine-in, delivery, catering, events

Slide 06
Traction

Soft launch results, customer reviews, early bookings

Slide 07
Financials

Revenue projections, break-even, 3-year forecast

Slide 08
The ₹30 Lakh Ask

Use of funds: kitchen upgrade, marketing, hiring, expansion

Slide 09
The Team

Founders, head chef credentials, advisory board

Slide 10
Next Steps

Investment terms, timeline, contact, call to action

Gamma Prompt: "Create a 10-slide investor pitch deck for Tandoor & Tales, an authentic Punjabi restaurant in Kotkapura, Punjab seeking ₹30 lakh in funding. Theme: warm, professional, appetizing. Include data placeholders where needed. Tone: confident, story-driven, investor-ready."
