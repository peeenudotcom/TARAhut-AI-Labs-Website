Section 02

Make a Comic Strip! 🎭

Create your own 4-panel comic with AI writing the story and AI drawing the art!

📋 How to Make Your Comic Strip

1

Write the Story

Tell AI: "Write a 4-panel comic story about a superhero student who saves their school from a homework monster. Give me Panel 1, 2, 3, 4 descriptions."

2

Create Each Panel Image

Take each panel description to Canva or Bing Image Creator. Type it as an art prompt to generate the picture for that panel. 🎨

3

Arrange in Canva

Open Canva → Create a new design → "Comic Strip" template → Add your 4 images in order → Add the text/speech bubbles!

4

Your Comic is Ready!

Download, print, or share on WhatsApp. You're now a real comic creator! 🦸‍♂️✨
