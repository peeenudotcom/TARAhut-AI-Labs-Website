Professional Invoicing

Create Professional Invoices

A professional invoice is your first impression as a business. It tells the client you are serious, organized, and trustworthy. No more 'bhai, paise bhej do' on WhatsApp.

🧾

Every Invoice Must Include

Your name/business name · Client name & address · Invoice number (INV-001, INV-002...) · Date & due date · Service description with line items · Total amount with GST if applicable · Payment method (UPI/Bank details) · Payment terms (Net 7 / Net 15)

📝Template

One-Time Project

For website builds, GMB setup, single campaigns. Clear scope, fixed price, 50% advance + 50% on delivery.

🔄Template

Monthly Retainer

For ongoing social media, content, maintenance. Monthly billing, auto-renewal, scope defined per month.

Hands-On: Create Your Invoice Template

  • Open Canva → Search "Invoice" → Pick a clean, professional template
  • Add your name/logo, placeholder client details, and sample services
  • Include UPI QR code or bank details at the bottom
  • Add payment terms: "Payment due within 7 days of invoice date"
  • Save as PDF — this is your reusable template
💡

Pricing Rule of Thumb

Never quote per hour. Always quote per project or per month. Hourly pricing punishes you for being fast. A GMB setup that takes you 2 hours is still worth ₹3,000-5,000 to the client because of the VALUE it delivers.

Income & Tax Basics

Track Every Rupee

If you don't track your money, you don't have a business — you have a hobby. Set up a simple system today that takes 5 minutes per week to maintain.

Hands-On: Income Tracking Spreadsheet

Open Google Sheets and create these columns:

  • Date — When payment received
  • Client Name — Who paid
  • Service — What you delivered (GMB, Website, Social Media, etc.)
  • Invoice # — Link to your invoice
  • Amount (₹) — How much
  • Status — Paid / Pending / Overdue
  • Payment Method — UPI / Cash / Bank Transfer
🏛️

Tax Basics for Indian Freelancers

Under ₹20L/year: No GST registration needed. Over ₹20L/year: GST registration mandatory (18% on services). ITR filing: File as individual under "Income from Business/Profession." Keep records: Save ALL invoices, payment screenshots, and expense receipts. TDS: If a client deducts TDS (10%), get Form 16A from them.

Calculate Your Break-Even

  • Monthly expenses: Phone (₹500) + Internet (₹800) + Tools (₹500) + Transport (₹1,000) = ~₹2,800
  • Your minimum monthly income target: ₹2,800 (expenses) + ₹7,200 (your salary) = ₹10,000
  • To earn ₹10K/month: 2 clients at ₹5,000/month OR 1 client at ₹10K/month
  • Stretch target: 5 clients at ₹5,000 = ₹25,000/month

Time Management

Handle 5 Clients Without Burnout

The #1 reason freelancers fail is not lack of clients — it's lack of systems. You get 3 clients, feel overwhelmed, drop quality, lose clients. Time blocking prevents this.

Weekly Time Block Template (5 Clients)
🌅
Morning
9-12: Client work
→
🍱
Midday
12-1: Break
→
💻
Afternoon
1-4: Client work
→
📋
Evening
4-5: Admin & review
📅Mon-Tue

Client A + B

Dedicate Mon-Tue mornings to 2 clients. Batch similar tasks: all content on Monday, all scheduling on Tuesday.

📅Wed-Thu

Client C + D

Same pattern. Wed-Thu for next 2 clients. Batching = fewer context switches = faster delivery.

📅Fri

Client E + Admin

Friday: 5th client in morning, admin in afternoon. Send invoices, update trackers, plan next week.

🚫

Boundaries You MUST Set

Response time: Reply within 4 business hours, NOT instantly. Scope: "This is included, this costs extra" — in writing. Availability: Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm. Weekend messages get Monday replies. Revisions: 2 revisions included, additional at ₹500 each.

Hands-On: Plan Your Week

  • Open Google Calendar or a paper planner
  • Block 9am-12pm and 1pm-4pm Mon-Fri for client work
  • Assign each client 1-2 dedicated days
  • Block 4pm-5pm daily for admin (invoices, emails, planning)
  • Block Saturday for new business development (not client work)

Upselling & Referrals

Grow Revenue from Existing Clients

Getting a NEW client costs 5x more effort than selling more to an EXISTING client. Your best source of revenue growth is the clients you already have.

📈Upsell

Add Services

Client has GMB only? Offer social media. Has social media? Offer a website. Has a website? Offer Google Ads. Always have the next service ready.

🤝Referral

Referral System

Happy client = free marketing. Ask for referrals at the right time: after delivering a win. Offer 10% discount on next month for every successful referral.

Referral Request Script

Use this after delivering results:

"[Client name] ji, I'm glad the [GMB/social media/website] is working well for you. If you know any other business owner who could benefit from similar services, I'd love to help them too. As a thank you, I'll give you 10% off next month's fee for every referral who signs up."
🎯

The Upsell Timing Rule

Wait until you've delivered a measurable result BEFORE upselling. 'Your GMB got 200 views this month — imagine what social media could add.' Results first, then pitch. Never upsell before delivering value.

"Tumhara pehla client tumhara sabse important asset hai. Usko itna khush karo ki woh tumhare liye bina maange 3 aur clients le aaye. That's how real businesses grow."
