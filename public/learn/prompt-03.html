Temperature — Controlling AI Heat

Temperature — AI की Heat कंट्रोल

Temperature — AI ਦੀ Heat ਕੰਟਰੋਲ

Temperature controls randomness. Low temperature = predictable, deterministic. High temperature = creative, surprising. Like a chef: low heat for precise cooking, high heat for bold experimentation. "Low temperature = recipe exactly follow karo. High temperature = nava kujh try karo."

There is no "best" temperature. There is only the RIGHT temperature for YOUR task.
TEMP 0❄️

The Cautious Chef

Always picks the highest-probability token. Run the same prompt 10 times — get the same output 10 times. Best for: Code generation, factual Q&A, data extraction, translations. "Jado accuracy zaruri hai — temp 0."

TEMP 0.3-0.5🍳

The Professional Chef

Mostly predictable with slight variation. Maintains quality while adding nuance. Best for: Business emails, reports, documentation, client communication. "Professional kaam layi sahi balance."

TEMP 0.7-1.0🔥

The Creative Chef

Considers less probable tokens. Output varies significantly between runs. Best for: Creative writing, brainstorming, poetry, marketing copy, storytelling. "Jado creativity chahidi hai."

TEMP 1.5-2.0🥵

The Experimental Chef

Near-random token selection. Often produces incoherent or surprising results. Best for: Experimental art, finding unexpected angles, creative exploration. "Pagal chef — interesting but risky."

Section 02

Beyond Temperature — Fine-Tuning Knobs

Temperature is the main dial. But there are 4 more knobs that give you precise control. "Temperature main knob hai — baaki 4 fine-tuning layi ne."

TOP-P🎯

Nucleus Sampling

Top-p 0.1: Only considers the top 10% most probable tokens. Very restricted. Top-p 0.9: Considers top 90%. Wide range. Like temperature but as a percentage. Pro tip: Use EITHER temperature OR top-p, not both. They can conflict.

FREQUENCY PENALTY🔄

Anti-Repetition

Penalizes tokens that have already appeared. Higher penalty = less repetition. 0: No penalty (might repeat phrases). 1-2: Strong penalty (avoids repeating words). Great for long-form content where AI tends to be repetitive.

PRESENCE PENALTY🌱

Topic Explorer

Encourages new topics by penalizing tokens from topics already discussed. 0: Stays on topic. 1-2: Branches into new territory. Useful for brainstorming sessions where you want diverse ideas.

MAX TOKENS✂️

Length Limiter

Hard cap on response length. If you set max_tokens to 150, the response stops at 150 tokens even mid-sentence. Use case: Control costs, enforce brevity, prevent rambling. "Jawab di length control karo."

💡

The Practical Truth

In daily use, temperature is 90% of what you need. Top-p, frequency penalty, and presence penalty are fine-tuning knobs for specific edge cases. Don't overwhelm yourself — master temperature first, then experiment with others. "Pehle temperature master karo, baaki baad vich."
